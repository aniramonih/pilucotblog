Jack Osbourne is a famous media personality, both in England and the United States of America, mostly due to a family TV reality series The Osbournes that aired on MTV from 2002 until 2005. After the show ended, Jack moved on to pursue personal interests, mostly related to traveling and fitness. His lifestyle and personality is shown in both Jack Osbourne: Adrenaline Junkie that was on TV in a period from 2005 to 2009, as well as Saving Planet Earth (2007) on BBC. The diagnosis of relapsing-remitting multiple sclerosis in 2012 changed his life and forced him to make significant lifestyle changes.

Education

Since the whole Osbourne family moved often during Jack’s childhood, he often had to change schools. He soon started despising going to school, and the feeling was affected by the diagnosis of ADHD and dyslexia at the time.

First, he went to a Christian school in Los Angeles but briefly went back to England. About a year later, he enrolled in the special needs Park Century School, also in Los Angeles.

Later, he expressed his desire to go back to school to get a master’s in history or anthropology.
