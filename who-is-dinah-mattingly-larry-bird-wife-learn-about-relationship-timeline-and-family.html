Who Is Dinah Mattingly Larry Bird Wife? Learn About Relationship Timeline And Family

Larry Bird is an enthusiastic American former professional basketball player married to his loving wife Dinah Mattingly since 1989. 

Larry Joe Bird, aka Larry Bird, is a well-known American former professional basketball player, coach, and executive in the National Basketball Association (NBA).

His nickname “the Hick from French Lick” and “Larry Legend” is also known by his nickname. The former athlete is widely considered one of the greatest basketball players of all time.

Bird grew up in French Lick, Indiana, where he was a local basketball phenom. Highly recruited, Larry initially agreed to play for coach Bobby Knight of the Indiana Hoosiers; however, he left after one month and returned to French Lick.

Meet Larry Bird Wife Dinah Mattingly: Relationship Timeline

Former professional basketball athlete Larry Bird is hitched to his better half, Dinah Mattingly. After dating for quite a while, the lovely couple finally exchanged their wedding vows in 1989.

Larry and his wife Dinah have two adopted children, Conner Bird and Mariah Bird. Dinah is the second wife of the former athlete. He was previously married to Janet Condra in 1975.

Unfortunately, the marriage couldn’t last long, and the couple remained married for less than a year. However, following an attempted reconciliation, Larry and Janet had a daughter, Corrie Bird, in 1977.

Now, Larry is a proud father of his three children. His current wife, Dinah, was born in Vigo County, Indiana, on November 16, 1954. Like her husband, she also attended Indiana State University.

Larry and Dinah have known each other since sometime around that. But they did not start dating instantly, as the former athlete was in a relationship with Janet, with whom he married later.

Former Professional Basketball Player Larry Bird Family – His Ethnicity

On December 7, 1956, basketball player Larry Bird was born in West Baden Springs, Indiana to his supportive parents, Georgia and Claude Joseph “Joe” Bird, a veteran of the Korean War, a war fought between North Korea and South Korea from 1950 to 1953.

His parents were of Scottish, Irish, and some Native American descent on both sides of the family. He is among the six children of his parents, and he has four brothers and a sister.

The former athlete was raised in nearby French Lick, where his mom worked two jobs to support the family. Bird often says that being poor as a child still motivates him.

When Larry was just in high school, his parents, Georgia and Joe, separated, and sadly Joe committed suicide about a year later after the divorce. Bird took basketball as an escape from his family troubles.

Larry got his way starring for Springs Valley High School and averaging 31 points, 21 rebounds, and four assists as a senior to becoming the school’s scoring leader of all-time.

Where Is Larry Bird Now? More To Know About The Former Athlete

Currently, Larry Bird is enjoying his life away from basketball with his lovely family. Just recently, the NBA star stepped down as the president of the Indiana Pacers.

In a statement, Larry said, “I am in my 60s now, and I want to try other things away from basketball. I would like to do scouting for the Pacers, college, NBA, international, do some appearances, and stay to advise senior basketball management.”
