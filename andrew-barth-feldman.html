Professional Career

From a young age, musical theater impacted Andrew Barth Feldman. He had seen a rendition of Beauty and the Beast when he was three years old and had pursued acting in school, performing in local/school productions of great musicals such as Annie, Grease, and Rent.

He also invented the Broadway Whodunit murder mystery series, which stars musical theater players. Besides this, Barth also appeared for the first time on HSMTMTS in episode 6 and at 54 Below, where he performed along with Joshua Bassett.
