'A Memphis legend': Tributes pour in for Gangsta Boo after Three 6 Mafia member found dead at 43

MEMPHIS, TENNESSEE: Three 6 Mafia member Gangsta Boo died at age 43. Born as Lola Mitchell, the Memphis rapper was found dead on Sunday, January 1, 2023, around 4 pm. The 43-year-old's sudden death has caused an outpouring of grief on social media. Mitchell's label-mate DJ Paul K.O.M. broke the news of her death and paid his tributes in a heartfelt Instagram post showing the rapper deejaying. 

Although her cause of death has not been disclosed officially, TMZ reported it as a possible drug overdose. Mitchell joined Three 6 Mafia in the 1990s and was the second female member of the Memphis hip-hop group after K-9. However, the rapper left the group in the early 2000s to launch her own solo albums and multiple mixtapes.
