Jill Scott’s Weight Journey: Embracing Challenging Roles & Pushing Physical Limits

Jill Scott is an accomplished artist with multiple Grammy Awards, a New York Times best-selling poet, and a highly acclaimed actor. She fearlessly takes on challenging on-screen roles that push her physical boundaries.

One notable example was during the filming of Tyler Perry's 2007 film "Why Did I Get Married?" when she intentionally gained 30 pounds. However, Scott is not putting excessive pressure on herself to lose the weight she gained.

Instead, she takes a gradual and sensible approach, incorporating walks and healthier habits into her lifestyle. This is because her original intention was not to gain weight in the first place.

While Jill Scott has been advocating the importance of maintaining a healthy lifestyle, she does not support those who frequently pit women against other women.

Jill Scott's Motivation behind Her Weight Loss Journey

In a 2011 interview, Jill Scott opened up about her significant weight loss of 63 pounds since 2009. The driving force behind her transformation was her desire for better health and the ability to keep up with her son, Jett Hamilton Roberts. Scott said:

"I'm just trying to grow, man, and always I just want to be the best and most confident me I can be. And now I'm someone's role model for real. I just want to be a fun mom."

She worked with a personal trainer who pushed her to engage in biking, throwing balls, kickboxing, and boxing, allowing her to release aggression while achieving positive results.

Jill Scott appears with her son Jett Hamilton Roberts at the 6th Annual Brittiana "Smile For Life" Run/Walk at Kenneth Hahn Park & Trails on May 14, 2011, in Los Angeles, California. | Source: Getty Images

She also emphasized the importance of enjoying the process and not rushing for immediate results, recommending a slow and mindful approach to weight loss.

The acclaimed actress has resided in Los Angeles, which she loves for its weather, which allows her to ride her bike daily—an activity that brings her immense joy.

Jill Scott's Physique Drew Netizens' Reactions

While Jill Scott has advocated the importance of maintaining a healthy lifestyle, she does not support those who frequently pit women against other women. For instance, she was recently compared to pop singer Lizzo.

In 2022, a Twitter user posted a viral tweet comparing the appearances of Lizzo and Scott, suggesting that men would prefer to date Scott over Lizzo due to her "classy" appearance.

The post featured side-by-side images of Scott at a press event and Lizzo in a daring outfit, boarding a private jet. The tweet received backlash.

Many netizens eventually denounced this recent comparison. A fan defended Scott by pointing out that comparing Scott's body to Lizzo's shed light on the existence of a double standard in society's treatment of small and large-sized individuals.

Another fan tweeted that if Jill Scott dressed like Lizzo, both celebrities would receive similar treatment. The fan also noted that society feels "intimidated" by confident, plus-size women and that "fatphobia" persists.

Jill Scott attends The 59th Grammy Awards at Staples Center on February 12, 2017, in Los Angeles, California. | Source: Getty Images

More people expressed their views on the issue. Another netizen remarked that those who want plus-size women to "stay in their place" are still fatphobic.

Someone also noted that Scott's body shape faced less criticism than Lizzo, who doesn't conform to conventional norms as easily.

Additionally, another fan suggested that some men don't show interest in Lizzo but appreciate Scott because they objectify her, reiterating society's double standards.

Despite fans' mixed opinions, Scott responded to the user's original tweet. Instead of expressing anger, Scott quickly replied to the user's tweet and posted a GIF of comedian Kevin Hart mouthing the words, "Stop that."
