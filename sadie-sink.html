Who Is Stranger Things' Actress, Sadie Sink Is She Dating Anyone?

Are you a regular viewer of the science-fiction horror television series, Stranger Things on Netflix? Then you must be familiar with the character of Maxine Mayfield, which is played by American actress, Sadie Sink.

Moreover, she is famous for her performances in the show American Odyssey as Suzanne Ballard and as Daisy Carpenter in the CBS TV drama, Blue Bloods. Stick with us to find more details about her.

Owing to her acting skills, Sadie has a substantial fan base who are interested in learning more about her personal life. Is she dating someone or is she still single? Sink, who is 19 years old, is a single lady, just like many of her co-stars.

Portrays As Max In Netflix Series, Stranger Things

The 1.6 m tall Sink landed the main role of Maxine aka Max Mayfield in season two of the science-fiction horror television series, Stranger Things. It premiered on July 15, 2016, on Netflix. 

Well, Maxine is known as the tomboy who grabbed the attention of both Lucas and Dustin and is also Billy's younger stepsister. It stars an ensemble cast including Cara Buono, Millie Bobby Brown, Charlie Heaton, Caleb McLaughlin, Shannon Purser, Maya Hawke, and more.

Along with the series, she has done acting in television series like The Americans, Blue Bloods, American Odyssey, and Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt.

Bio and Family Life

Sadie Sink was born on 16 April 2002 in Brenham, Texas, United States. She is the daughter of Casey Adam Sink, a football coach and Lori Elizabeth Sink, a mathematics teacher.

Sink has four siblings, three older brothers named Caleb Sink, Spencer Sink and Mitchell Sink and one younger sister, Jacey Sink. Her brother Mitchell is also an actor. Moreover, she attended the Summit Senior High School in New Jersey.

At the age of 11, she started acting in the Broadway production of Annie.
