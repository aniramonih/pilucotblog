Days of Our Lives Spoilers: Bo &amp; Hopes Return Airdate See When Peter Reckell &amp; Kristian Alfon

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers reveal that the countdown is on when it comes to Bo Brady (Peter Reckell) and Hope Brady’s (Kristian Alfonso) long-awaited return. “Bope” fans are certainly eager to see the pair back in action, so let’s talk about some exciting news posted on Days’ social media account.

If you’ve been wondering about an exact date for Peter Reckell and Kristian Alfonso’s joint comeback, you’re in luck.

Days spoilers confirm it’s less than 30 days away, so it won’t be long at all until Bo and Hope are on our screens once again.

On Twitter, DOOL sent out a little promo that featured a blast-from-the-past Hope and Bo photo from 2011.

They also promised the return of this “epic love story” and revealed “Bope” would be back starting 30 days from February 14.

That puts Bo and Hope’s return airdate on March 16, so plenty of Days viewers are pumped about this duo’s new chapter.

It’ll be great to see these fan favorites become part of some fresh storylines.

On the Beyond Salem series, Bo was finally awakened in a cryogenic chamber thanks to Megan Hathaway’s (Miranda Wilson) use of the three prisms.

More recently on the main show, Susan Banks (Stacy Haiduk) – who turned out to be Nick Fallon (Blake Berris) in disguise – commented on the asterisk by Bo’s name in the afterlife.

It’s good to know Bo is getting a chance to return to his earthly life with Hope and his family.

Although Bo was always able to guide his loved ones from heaven, it was never quite the same as having him there in the flesh.

Now Bo is back for real and that opens lots of new story opportunities moving forward.

Bo and Hope have some terrific material coming up, so don’t miss the “Bope” reunion ahead.

Are you looking forward to Peter Reckell’s comeback as Bo Brady and Kristian Alfonso’s return as Hope Brady?

Days of Our Lives spoilers say March 16 will kick off a new “Bope” era, so stay tuned for updates on what’s in store!
