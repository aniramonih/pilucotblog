Peggy Lipton is an actress and model who rose to fame in the late 1960s and early 1970s with her role as Julie Barnes in the TV show The Mod Squad, for which she won a Golden Globe Award.

Peggy  is also known for her role as Norma Jennings in the cult series Twin Peaks and its revival.

Quincy Jones and Peggy Lipton met in 1971 and married in 1974.

However, their marriage was not easy, as they faced racism and prejudice from both sides of their families and society at large.
