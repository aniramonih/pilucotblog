Alex Trebek Raps Fresh Prince Theme Song on Jeopardy!: Video

Watch out, Will Smith! Longtime Jeopardy! host Alex Trebek rapped the lyrics to the popular Fresh Prince of Bel-Air theme song during the game show on Monday, June 29, and naturally, the moment has gone viral.

A contestant named Brandon decided to go for the "TV for $800" category.

"In the theme to this '90s sitcom," Trebek read aloud, before transitioning his intonation to mimic the original Smith-rapped theme song. "'I pulled up to the house about 7 or 8 and I yelled to the cabbie, Yo, homes, smell ya later!'"
