Drake &amp; Diddy Headed To St. Barts For LuisaViaRoma Unicef Gala

Drake and Diddy are gearing up to take a trip down to the Caribbean island of St. Barts for the annual LuisaViaRoma for Unicef gala.

According to a report from Page Six, Drizzy will be performing at the gala taking place at the lavish Emeraude Hotel on Thursday (December 29); Diddy will be one of 700 people at the charity event that aims to raise funds for UNICEF’s efforts around the world.

Lenny Kravitz will also take the stage as a performer. Other invited guests include Mary J. Blige, Naomi Watts, Billy Crudup, Luke Evans, Mike Tyson, Rita Ora, and Taika Waititi.

The charity event will also host an auction with Simon De Pury playing auctioneer who will auction off Steve McQueen’s race car and one of Elvis Presley’s prized motorcycles. Hospitality guru Richie Akiva is set to erecting the largest tent ever built on the beach and tables for the event have reportedly gone on sale for $250,000.

Last year’s event was attended by Lil Baby, Meek Mill, Mike Tyson, Leonardo DiCaprio, Kygo, Cuba Gooding Jr, Alec Monopoly, Dua Lipa and more.

Drake and Diddy are looking to close out the year with a bang after having both having a hugely successful 2022.
