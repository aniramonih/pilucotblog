Former NL MVP Chipper Jones is taken for the third time! His married life with model Taylor Higgins

Chipper Jones Exquisite Wedding...

After dating for three years, former NL MVP Chipper Jones tied the knot with his girlfriend Taylor Higgins on June 2015. Taylor is a former playboy model who goes by the name of Lexi Ray Taylor and posed nude for Playboy cyber on March 4, 2010.

Third time's the charm!!

Former baseball player understands the rules of the third strike. This is his third marriage and he hopes to be a good husband to his newlywed wife. Prior to Higgins, Jones tied the knot with Karin Kulford in 1992 after dating for almost two years.

During his marital years with Karin, the couple never had any children. However, Jones had a son, Matthew during that period with a Hooters waitress. Yes, he cheated on her which is also the reason for his divorce with Karin in 1999.

Not long after his split with Karin, he found love again in Sharon Logonov. After a year of dating, the couple performed their nuptials in March 2000. 

This is Jones longest relationship as he stayed together with Sharon for twelve years. From his second nuptials, he became the father to three children named, Larry Wayne III, Tristen Jones, and Shea Jones.
