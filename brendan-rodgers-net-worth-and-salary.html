Brendan Rodgers Net Worth And Salary

Brendan Rodgers is a highly-regarded football manager from Northern Ireland. He has managed several clubs in his career and has gained recognition for his tactical acumen and ability to develop young players.

Rodgers began his managerial career at the youth level in the Chelsea academy before moving to Reading, where he worked as a youth and reserve team coach.

In 2008, he was appointed as the manager of Watford, a Championship (second-tier) club in England. After a successful spell at Watford, Rodgers moved to Swansea City in 2010.
