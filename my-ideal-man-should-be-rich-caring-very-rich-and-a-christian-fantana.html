My Ideal Man Should Be Rich, Caring, Very Rich And A Christian Fantana

Rufftown records signee, Francine Nyanko Koffi popularly known as Fantana has revealed on Sammy Kay’s Go online show has talked about her ideal man

Fantana stated that she is blessed with everything and went ahead to give a checklist of her ideal man.

She said “My ideal man should be rich, caring, very rich and a Christian, i can manage the rest”.

That notwithstanding, she explained that whoever she falls in love with, whether rich or poor and still feels like building him up to the man she desires and want, she will help in that aspect in all honesty.

Talking about how she feels about her looks; According to the “Girls hate on girls” songstress, Fantana people are getting an injection for big lips and big mouth so if she is fortunate and blessed with big lips and a big mouth, she isn’t perturbed with insults channeled at her.
