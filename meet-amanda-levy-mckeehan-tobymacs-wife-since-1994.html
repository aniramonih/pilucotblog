TobyMac Has Become A Respected Christian Musician

Amanda Levy McKeehan’s husband, TobyMac, is now a highly respected American gospel artist known for his soulful voice, He has made significant contributions to contemporary Christian music as a singer, rapper, songwriter, and record producer. He gained prominence as a member of the Christian rap and rock trio DC Talk, remaining with them from 1987 until their hiatus in 2000.

Following the group’s hiatus, TobyMac embarked on a successful solo career, releasing a total of nine studio albums including Momentum (2001), Welcome to Diverse City (2004), Portable Sounds (2007), Tonight (2010), Christmas in Diverse City (2011), and others. TobyMac has also released two full-length Christmas albums: Christmas in Diverse City (2011) and Light Of Christmas (2017). Notably, his album Eye on It became the third Christian album to debut at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 chart.

So far, TobyMac has sold over 10 million albums and earned seven Grammy Awards, becoming one of the most successful gospel musicians of his time. He holds an impressive record of six No. 1 hit singles on the CHR charts, including “Gone,” “Made to Love,” and “Lose My Soul.” Furthermore, he has achieved six No. 1 singles on Billboard’s Christian Songs chart, solidifying his position as one of the artists with the most chart-topping hits.
