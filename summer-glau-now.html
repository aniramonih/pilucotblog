What Happened To Summer Glau? Where Is She In 2023?

In the early 2010s, Summer Glau was one of the most well-known figures in sci-fi and fantasy, having established herself with a number of cult-favorite shows and films that were tragically shortened.

Almost halfway through the previous decade, she suddenly drifted off to the stars when everything appeared to be in her favour for success. The good news is that she dictated the terms.

Summer Glau got off to a fast start in her career, appearing briefly in an episode of Angel before being chosen for the brief but adored Firefly role.

Even while her time on the Serenity crew wasn’t quite as lengthy as anybody had hoped, her portrayal of the enigmatic River Tam helped to make her a force to be reckoned with.

Summer didn’t allow the cancellation of the show hold her down, even if it was a regrettable reminder of Hollywood’s peculiar practises. She would eventually receive some satisfaction for the role in Serenity, the feature-length sequel.

The following ten years saw Glau solidify her place in the business by beginning her acting career with made-for-television films and guest roles in criminal procedurals, much like the majority of other up-and-coming performers at the time.

She didn’t obtain a part to match her tenure as River Tam until 2008 when she was cast as Cameron in Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles.

Glau gained plaudits for her part in the series, one of the first to attempt retconning a prior Terminator movie, along with a pre-Game of Thrones Lena Heady.

Sadly, the show was cancelled after two seasons in a manner similar to how Firefly was treated.

With a strong will to succeed, Glau was able to star in a number of other niche shows with devoted fan bases, such as The Cape and Alphas.

Sadly, these were likewise ended way too soon, with The Cape even turning into a Community meta-joke of its own.

She eventually became so well-known among geek fans that she got herself a guest spot on The Big Bang Theory!

What Happened To Summer Glau?

After having her first child with her future husband Val Morrison in 2015, Glau decided to pull away from the spotlight. She had continued to find success in recurring parts like Ravager in Arrow and even had an appearance in Castle alongside her former Serenity captain Nathan Fillion.

Since then, the couple has had their second child, and they were also legally wed in 2017.

This Is What Summer Glau Is Doing Now

For the time being, Summer says she’s content to keep working on her most fulfilling project to date—becoming a mother.

According to Glau, she homeschools both of her kids and is committed to continuing to look out for their long-term wellbeing and education.

She still makes time for her followers, though, making frequent appearances at expos and conventions. She still values her followers twenty years after her breakthrough, that much is certain.

Glau has stated that she would like to reprise some of her fans’ favourite characters, despite the fact that she has insisted there is currently nothing in the works.

The actress expressed a wish to pick up where The Sarah Connor Chronicles left off more than ten years ago and continue the story as Cameron in a recent interview. This would give the character some closure.

Even if it’s unlikely, a sequel wouldn’t be too far-fetched by modern Terminator standards.

The star is right now preoccupied with her family and the near future, choosing to seize those brief, fleeting moments before they vanish like leaves in the wind.

Fans can find comfort in the fact that she is living her best life in the interim and that she is garnering more and more support for her potential return, even though she might not be on television anytime soon.
