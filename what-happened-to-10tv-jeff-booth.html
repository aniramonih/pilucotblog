What Happened To 10TV Jeff Booth: Where Is He Now? Leaving Rumors And New Job

Let’s find out “What Happened To 10TV Jeff Booth?” The weather anchor for 10TV WBNS, American journalist and meteorologist Jeff Booth has left the company. What went wrong with him?

In Columbus, Ohio, 10TV WBNS employed Booth, a professional meteorologist from the United States, as a weathercaster. He was hired by West Virginia’s WOAY in the beginning of 2002 for his first full-time television position.

While in Hawaii, he obtained his accreditation from the American Meteorological Society and the National Weather Association. Jeff was also the program’s first meteorologist and traffic reporter when “Sunrise on KGMB” debuted in 2007.

Similarly, in 2009, Jeff’s family was growing, and he decided to return to his home state. As a result, he was offered the Chief Meteorologist position at WKEF and WRGT in Dayton, Ohio. Despite all the moving, Columbus remained a treasured part of his life.

What Happened To 10TV Jeff Booth: Where Is He Now? Leaving Rumors

Jeff Booth recently announced on his Facebook page that he is leaving 10TV and the broadcasting industry for the time being.

The weathercaster on 10TV WBNS has recently been absent from the show. Following that, viewers of the show were left wondering where he went, with some speculating health issues.

According to his statement, he has enjoyed providing the viewers with the weather every morning, but he needs to take a break, spend some time with his family, and get some much-needed rest.

Additionally, he thanks the audience for its support. Also, all of the coworkers he has worked with over the years.

After the announcement, many of his fans expressed sadness and said they would miss him. Others wished him the best of luck in his future endeavors.

Jeff Booth New Job: What Happened To Him?

As of yet, Jeff Booth has not officially announced his new job. His most recent post hasn’t mentioned his next step after leaving 10TV.

Similarly, he has stated that he will be taking a break from broadcasting. He has only mentioned resting and spending time with his family. Booth is a husband and father of three daughters.

The family continues to live in Columbus, Ohio. When it comes to his personal life, Jeff is a private person. He likes to keep most of his family life out of the public eye.

Many people discussed on Reddit why Jeff Booth was no longer working for WBNS and if they had dumped him. However, it doesn’t appear to be the case.

Since he has had a long working experience, he must have made some plans for the next step.

Before joining WOAY, he was employed by WLIO in Lima, Ohio, where he handled weather duties on weekday mornings. After that, he relocated to the middle of the Pacific Ocean when Jeff was a weekend weather anchor and reporter at KGMB in Honolulu, Hawaii.
