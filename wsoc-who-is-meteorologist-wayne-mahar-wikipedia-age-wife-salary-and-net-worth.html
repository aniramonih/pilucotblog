Who Is Meteorologist Wayne Mahar? WSOC Broadcaster Wikipedia Details 

Wayne is popularly known for working in the CNY central for 37 years of his life. He is the former chief meteorologist of the station. 

Besides his broadcasting career, Mahar is also the president and owner of the Precision Weather Service and Priscilla Mahar Animal Welfare Foundation.
