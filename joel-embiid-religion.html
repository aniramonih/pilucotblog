Embiid was discovered at a basketball program by fellow Yaoundé native and NBA player Luc Mbah a Moute. Embiid relocated to the United States at the age of 16 to pursue a career as a professional basketball player, with Mbah a Moute as his mentor.

Embiid attended Mbah a Moute’s alma mater, Montverde Academy, for one year before transferring due to a lack of playing time. In Gainesville, Florida, he attended The Rock School, a Christian boarding school.
