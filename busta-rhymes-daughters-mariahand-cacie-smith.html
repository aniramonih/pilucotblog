Busta Rhymes Daughters: Mariah Smith And Cacie Smith

Mariah Smith and Cacie Smith are the daughters of the renowned American rapper Busta Rhymes. The Smith sisters were born around the late 1990s. Thus, both of them are in their mid-twenties.

Mariah completed her high school education in June 2017. Her father congratulated her on her graduation through a social media post.

“Congrats to my amazing daughter Mariah on her high school graduation. I am extremely proud of you, and I love you.” The “Make It Clap” hitmaker added, “Time to keep it going and keep it growing. Your big brother TZ is right there to ensure you are good.”

Though her current whereabouts are a bit blurry, Mariah must have already graduated from one of the most prestigious universities and doing her dream job.

On the other hand, Cacie Smith, the rapper’s other daughter, is a graduate of Quinnipiac University. Cacie is reported to be a model.

She is active on Instagram under the username @ca.cie, where she introduced herself as an artist in Los Angeles, California. The gorgeous lady has a massive fan following (26.1K) on her social media account.
