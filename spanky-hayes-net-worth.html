What is Spanky Hayes' Net Worth?

Spanky Hayes is an American actor, comedian, and writer who has a net worth of $300,000. He was born Carlos Hayes in Detroit, Michigan on January 31, 1986. He has worked on projects like All Jokes Aside, Nick Cannon Presents: Wild 'N Out, Tons of Love, Baggin', Jamie Foxx Presents: America's Funniest Comics: Vol. 3, and Wild 'N Out: Wildest Moments. He made headlines in 2014 for alleging there is a "secret black gay world" in the midst of Hollywood naming several high profile African American men as people who have taken part in order to advance their careers.
