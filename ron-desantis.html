    Ron DeSantis Facts

  • In 2015, he founded the Freedom Caucus, a group of ultra conservative Republican members from the House of Representatives.
  • Ron is a Trumpist (ardent supporter of Donald Trump).
  • At the age of 40, he became the youngest Governor of Florida in more than 100 years.
  • DeSantis passed the highly-controversial ‘Don’t Say Gay’ law in March 2022, which prohibited any conversations about s**ual orientation and gender identity among school kids from kindergarten to grade 3.

  • Ron is pro-life. In 2022, he signed the contentious 15-week abortion ban in Florida.
  • In 2021, he banned Florida public schools from teaching students about critical race theory.
