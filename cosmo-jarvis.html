Cosmo Jarvis

Birth Name: Harrison Cosmo Krikoryan Jarvis

Place of Birth: Ridgewood, New Jersey, U.S.

Date of Birth: 1 September, 1989

Ethnicity:
*English (father)
*Armenian (mother)

Cosmo Jarvis is an American-British actor, musician, and filmmaker. His roles include the films Lady Macbeth (2017), Farming, Calm with Horses, Funny Face, and Persuasion (2022), and the series Peaky Blinders and Raised by Wolves.

He was born in Ridgewood, New Jersey, to an English father and American mother, who is of Armenian ancestry. He moved to England as a young child, living in Totnes, Devon.

Cosmo’s maternal grandfather was named Ashod N. Krikoryan. Ashod was born in Istanbul, Turkey.

Cosmo’s maternal grandmother was named Arsho.
