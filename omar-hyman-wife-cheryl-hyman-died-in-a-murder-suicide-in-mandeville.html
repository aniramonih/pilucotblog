Tragic Domestic Incident Claims Lives of Husband and Wife: St. Tammany Authorities Investigate

A heartbreaking domestic incident has left a husband-and-wife dead in St. Tammany Parish. Authorities report that Omar Hyman, 44, lost his life after being shot during a confrontation with his wife, Cheryl Hyman, 41, before seeking refuge in a neighbor’s residence.

The St. Tammany Parish Sheriff’s Office responded promptly to the distress call, arriving at the scene on Cherry Ridge Court shortly before 7:20 a.m. on Monday. Preliminary investigations indicate that the tragic incident was the result of a domestic dispute that turned violent.

Upon their arrival, deputies learned that Omar Hyman had sustained gunshot wounds inside his residence following the altercation with his wife. In his attempt to escape, Omar Hyman sought assistance from a neighbor, who immediately called 911 and provided aid. Tragically, Omar Hyman’s injuries proved fatal, and he was transported to a local hospital, where he later succumbed to his wounds.

The St. Tammany Parish Sheriff’s Office took swift action, dispatching the SWAT team to the scene. As efforts to establish contact with Cheryl Hyman proved unsuccessful, law enforcement entered the couple’s residence, where they discovered Cheryl Hyman deceased from an apparent gunshot wound.

The investigation into this heartrending incident is currently underway. Authorities are working diligently to uncover the circumstances surrounding the tragedy. The identities of the victims, Omar and Cheryl Hyman, have been released by STPSO, with family members already informed.
