Jo Koy Has a Son: Who Is The Mother?

If you are a big fan of Jo Koy, you know that he has a son whom he loves a lot. His son's name is Joseph, and he is also very active on Instagram. The proud father likes to talk about his son and how grateful he is to have him. 

However, the question about the mother of the child is an often raised question. Koy has not revealed any information about the mother of his child. But not long ago, during an interview, he admitted that he was married to a mystery woman and had his son with her. 

But as things went downhill in their marriage, he separated from her and got the authority of his son. The terms of the split are still unknown, but it seems they got a divorce under mutual understandings. 

During an interview, the comedian shared that he was very grateful for his ex-wife because she gave him the most important thing in his life which is his son. 

He said:

I am so thankful for the mother of my son. She’s given me the most important person in my life – my son. I thank her for taking good care of him while I’m away and on the road, being a good mom to him and friend to me.

While Koy has not revealed any information about his son's mother, Joseph, on the other hand, did upload a picture of his mother on Instagram wishing her on her birthday.
