Charles Dance is a famous English actor, screenwriter, and film director. One of Charles' recognizable roles was Tywin Lannister in the HBO's series, 'Game of Thrones.' As of recent, he also played Lord Mountbatten in the web TV series, 'The Crown.'

Charles Dance was born as Walter Charles Dance on October 10, 1946, in Redditch, Worcestershire. He is the son of Walter Dance and his wife, Elanor Marion. His father was an electrical engineer and served in the Second Boer War in South Africa, and his mother was a cook. Charles's father died when he was just three years old. He completed his studies from Leicester College with a Degree in Graphic Design and Photography.
