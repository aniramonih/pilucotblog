Jenner Hints at Pregnancy in Instagram Post

Are we going to see another KUWTK baby soon? This star just hinted they’re ready to expand their family, and we are here for it! In an adorable Instagram post, they asked if fans were ready for them to add another one to the ever-expanding Kardashian/Jenner tribe.

With Kourtney on her way out, and Khloe discussing the possibility of a spinoff, we might be heading in the direction of individual family shows, and this star is ready to add to the growing third generation of this powerful family!

As for us, we’re just ready to find out the baby’s name.
