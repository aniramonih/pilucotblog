Bachelorette' 2023 

The twentieth season of The Bachelorette premiered on June 26, 2023, featuring 27-year-old Charity Lawson, a child and family therapist from Columbus, Georgia. Prior to becoming the Bachelorette, Charity had previously appeared on the 27th season of The Bachelor, where she finished in fourth place, competing for the heart of Zach Shallcross.

Article continues below advertisementArticle continues below advertisement

Her journey as the Bachelorette began with the exciting announcement made by Jesse Palmer during The Bachelor's Women Tell All special on March 14, 2023.

The season promises to be filled with interesting contestants, including notable individuals like WWE NXT wrestler Caleb Balgaard, Guinness World Record holder Chris Spell, and Sean McLaughlin, the son of politician Steven McLaughlin. These diverse contestants are sure to bring unique personalities and experiences to the show.

Filming for the season commenced on March 20, 2023, in Agoura Hills, California, and continued with additional shoots in different locations. Charity and the contestants traveled to New Orleans, Louisiana, for some exciting dates and memorable moments. The season's journey reached its conclusion in Fiji later in April, where the final stages of the show were filmed, leading up to the season finale set to air on August 21, 2023.

With a mix of romantic encounters, emotional connections, and dramatic twists, viewers are in for an unforgettable season as they follow Charity on her quest to find true love among the diverse group of contestants vying for her heart. As the episodes unfold, fans will witness the journey of love, heartbreak, and ultimate happiness as Charity searches for her perfect match in 'The Bachelorette' Season 20.
