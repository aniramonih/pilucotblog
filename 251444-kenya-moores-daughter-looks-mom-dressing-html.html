Kenya Moore's Daughter Looks like Mom after Dressing Herself in a Pink Coat with Matching Boots

Reality TV star Kenya Moore's two-year-old daughter Brooklyn Daly is a mini version of her mom as she dresses in a pink hooded jacket with matching boots.

Kenya Moore's daughter Brooklyn Doris Daly is at an age where kids begin to express themselves, and for every parent, this period is arguably the most fun part of parenthood.

And Moore, who loves to dote on her baby girl, has created a personal Instagram account for Brooklyn, where she regularly documents some of these special moments.

Kenya Moore visited "Extra" at the Levi's store in Times Square, New York on November 4, 2019. | Photo: Getty Images

The proud mom recently shared a cute picture of her daughter on the toddler's personal Instagram page. In the photo, the little princess is wearing a pink hooded jacket with matching pink boots.

Brooklyn's hair is packed into a bun, and she bears a striking resemblance with her mom as she flashes a beautiful smile while posing for the camera. Moore captioned the photo:

"I dressed myself but I'm not sure who will let me in without pants or a shirt but I'm sure I'll find out soon."

Moore and her husband, Marc Daly, welcomed their daughter Brooklyn on November 4, 2018. The couple named their daughter in reference to the location they met and fell in love.

The "The Real Housewives of Atlanta" star, who underwent IVF to conceive her daughter, had to overcome some additional hurdles before finally giving birth to her "miracle baby" at 47.

This will make it the second time the couple would attempt to end their marriage following their split in September 2019.

Being a working mom, Moore has managed to develop a close bond with her daughter, and she regularly flaunts their sweet mother-daughter relationship on her Instagram page.

Last year, the novel coronavirus pandemic-induced lockdown allowed Moore to spend more quality time with her daughter. And with things slowly returning to normal, Moore claimed she was not looking forward to leaving her daughter for work. In her words:

"really enjoyed bonding with [Brooklyn] and seeing every moment, and I've gotten our routine down. It's been really amazing..."

While Moore is enjoying every moment of motherhood, her marriage has been on the brink of collapse since 2019. In January, Daly announced that he and Moore have agreed to end their marriage.

Moore and Daly tied the knot in June 2017 at a very intimate ceremony in St. Lucia that had in attendance the couple's close friends and family members.

This will make it the second time the couple would attempt to end their marriage following their split in September 2019. However, the couple reconciled and unsuccessfully tried to repair their marriage through therapy.

There have been no reports of either of them filing for divorce yet. And while their fans would be praying for another reconciliation, the couple remains focused on doing what is best for their daughter.
