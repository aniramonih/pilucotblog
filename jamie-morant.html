Who Is Jamie Morant's Husband?

Jamie Morant's husband, Tee Morant, a former student-athlete, played basketball during his high school years and continued his sports endeavors at Claflin University, where he met Jamie. To sustain the family, Tee ventured into barbering and later established the Tee Morant Skills & Agility Training Camp. His influence on Ja's basketball career was significant as he became Ja's initial coach, starting his training in basketball during Ja's childhood. Tee's coaching style, though tough, was credited with shaping Ja into the formidable attacking point guard he is known as today with the Memphis Grizzlies.
