Sharon Stone health: What disease does Sharon Stone have?

Actress Sharon Stone used her Instagram Stories to urge followers to prioritise their health after going through excruciating agony.

The 64-year-old stated she was given the incorrect treatment (two epidurals) and a false diagnosis in her post on social media. She made mention of finally seeking a second opinion, which indicated she had a “big fibroid tumour that needs to come out,” however she did not specify what her previous (incorrect) diagnosis was.

In terms of her health, Stone hasn’t had it easy. The actress recently said that although she underwent surgery to remove breast malignancies, she awoke with bigger breast implants than she had originally agreed to. According to Variety, Stone suffered a brain haemorrhage and stroke the same year after getting a terrible headache.
