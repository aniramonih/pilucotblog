Shin Min Ah is not her real name

In a twist of fate, during her middle school years, Min-a participated in a school picnic where she and her friends took a photo together. Impressed by her appearance, she decided to send the picture to the teen magazine KiKi. Little did she know that this simple act would launch her modeling career.
