BABY JOY!Tom Arnold and Ashley Groussman Welcome a Baby Girl

Congrats Tom Arnold and Ashley Groussman!

The 56-year-old actor and his wife welcomed their second child, a baby girl named Quinn Sophie, on Friday, Dec. 18, according to E! News.

“Our second miracle has arrived and she is perfect,” the couple told the publication regarding their newest bundle of joy, who reportedly weighed in at 7 pounds and 9 oz.

Tom and Ashley in June 2015.

This big news is especially heartwarming, considering Tom recently revealed he had gone through numerous rounds of in vitro fertilization with former wives Roseanne Barr, Julia Armstrong and Shelby Roos.

“I didn’t think it was in the cards for me, to be honest, and so I was completely shocked,” he told The View after discovering his now wife was expecting. “It’s a miracle.”

The duo — who tied the knot in 2009 — are already parents to two-year-old son, Jax Copeland.

Congratulations to the happy couple on their family’s newest addition!
