Canelo Career

Saul “Canelo” Alvarez, a Mexican professional boxer, has had a remarkable career with achievements across various weight divisions.

Canelo Alvarez turned professional at the young age of 15 in 2005. He gained attention for his impressive record and powerful punching style.

By 2010, he had become a well-known name in boxing, holding an undefeated record.

Canelo secured his first world championship in March 2011 by defeating Matthew Hatton to win the WBC Light Middleweight title.

He defended the title multiple times, solidifying his status as a dominant force in his weight class.

Alvarez faced Floyd Mayweather Jr. in a high-profile bout in September 2013.

Although he lost the fight, it gave him valuable experience against one of the greatest boxers ever.

He won against top opponents like Alfredo Angulo and Erislandy Lara during this period.

Canelo moved to the middleweight division and captured the WBO and WBA titles.

He engaged in a highly anticipated showdown with Gennady Golovkin, resulting in a controversial draw in 2017 and a close victory for Canelo in the rematch in 2018.

These fights cemented his status as one of the best middleweights in the world.

Canelo continued increasing weight and won the WBA “Regular” Super Middleweight title in December 2018.

He later unified the division by defeating Callum Smith to add the WBC and The Ring titles. He also claimed the WBO Light Heavyweight title in November 2019.

Canelo’s impressive run continued as he defended his super middleweight titles against Avni Yildirim and Billy Joe Saunders. These victories solidified his position as a dominant force in the division.

In November 2021, Canelo Alvarez achieved a historic milestone by becoming the undisputed super middleweight champion.

He defeated Caleb Plant to unify all four major titles in the division, making him the first Mexican-born boxer to achieve this feat.

He is a multiple-time world champion across different weight divisions.

He is one of Mexico’s most successful and celebrated boxers. He is recognized for his impressive knockout power and boxing skills.
