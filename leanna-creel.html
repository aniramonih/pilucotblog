Let's Talk About Leanna Cree

Leanna Cree is a multi-talented American personality who is known among the public as an American actress, producer, director, and screenwriter. She came into popularity after she portrayed her role as Lisa Wyatt in the Parent Trap: Hawaiian Honeymoon which was released in the year 1989.

She was born on August 27, 1970, in Los Angeles, California, the United States of America, therefore her nationality is American and she holds Virgo as her birth sign.
