Joyce Meyer Arrested: What Were The Charges? Everything To Know About The American Christian Author

Joyce Meyer Arrested: Joyce Meyer and her ministries had been charged in court, but the charges were dropped in 2013. What were the allegations?

Joyce Meyer is a speaker and author. She is currently the president of Joyce Meyer Ministries, which is based in the Fenton suburb of St. Louis.

Meyer has also served as a pastor and is a popular bible teacher. Her show “Enjoying Everyday Life” helped her gain even more popularity.

Meyer has also published a large number of books during her lifetime. Battlefield of Mind, published in 1997, is one of her most well-known works.

On What Charges Was Joyce Meyer Arrested? 

Joyce Meyer and her ministries were facing death charges over Christopher Coleman’s murder in 2011.

On May 5. 2009, her Chief of Security, Coleman was arrested for killing his wife and sons. He was found guilty of three-count of murders in 2011.

Apparently, Meyer had to appear in the trials of Coleman. She provided pre-recorded testimony in the trial session.

Also, the family of Coleman’s wife had filed a wrongful-death lawsuit against her ministries. They blame Meyer for her negligence as a counselor.

However, the lawsuit was dismissed by Judge Richard Aguirre in 2013. The televangelist has never been arrested before.

Joyce Meyer Has A Huge Net Worth 

Joyce Meyer has a net worth of $8 million in 2021. As per her Wikipedia bio, she owns several homes and a private jet.

Meyer had accumulated her massive wealth from TV. She was even listed the 17th on Time’s Magazine as the 25 Most Influential Evangelicals.

In past, the televangelist has also been criticized for her extravagant lifestyle.

Additionally, she also collects up huge revenues from the sale of her books and from their ministry.

Joyce Meyer Is Married To Husband Dave Meyer

Joyce Meyer married her husband Dave Meyer in the year 1967. They were introduced in a local bar.

Dave has worked as an engineering draftsman in past. At, present, he serves as the Vice President of Joyce Meyer Ministries.

He is 79 years old, born on 31st July 1940 in the United States. In 1993, it was Dave who suggested that they start a television ministry.

The couple is parents to four grown children. They are Daniel B. Meyer, David Meyer, Sandra Ellen McCollom, and Laura Marie Holtzmann.

Both David and Daniel are part of the Board of Directors at Joyce Meyer Ministries.
