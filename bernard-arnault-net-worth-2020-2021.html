Early Life & Biography

Arnault was born on five march 1949 in Roubaix, France. He is the son of Jean Leon Arnault, who had a civil engineering company, Ferrel-Savinel. Arnault attended one of the most prestigious engineering schools found in France École Polytechnique in Palaiseau, graduating in an M.Sc. degree in 1971. After his graduation, he joined his father’s company in 1976 and succeeded him for the presidency in 1979.

Personal Life
