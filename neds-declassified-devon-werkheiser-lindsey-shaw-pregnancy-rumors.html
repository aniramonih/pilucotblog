Neds Declassified co-stars Devon Werkheiser and Lindsey Shaw spark pregnancy rumors with Littl

TWO child stars have set rumors flowing with a cryptic new announcement post.

Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide star Devon Werkheiser, 32, sparked pregnancy rumors with a photo on Instagram on Monday.

In the post on his Stories, Devon - who played Ned in the show - posed with his co-star Lindsey Shaw, 34, who played his best pal Moze.

The photo showed the duo posing together in the studio, both smiling at the camera.

Lindsay looked glam but relaxed in a tight green dress, showing off the curves of her figure.

The television star wore her long hair down, and wore only a tiny bit of mascara.

SAY WHAT?

While the duo's announcement hasn't been confirmed as pregnancy, they've been dropping hints of romance for a long time.

In a recent post on his Instagram Stories, Devon shared a clip in which he and Lindsey spoke about their fans' speculations - and added more fuel to the fire.

"Does it ever get weird for you, Lindsey, how much the audience ships us?"

"No," said Lindsey, while Devon agreed, 'Cool, me neither."
