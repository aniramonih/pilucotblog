Early Life And Education

Aron Accurso is an American composer and music director, born on 26 February 1979 in Denver, Colorado.

He attended St. Olaf College, where he studied music and honed his skills as a composer and music director.

Through his various roles, Accurso has significantly impacted the world of musical theater and continues to be a highly sought-after professional in the industry.

Aron Accurso has an impressive background in musical direction and incidental music arrangement. He has previously worked on projects such as “Up Her,” a musical by Bobby Lopez and Kristen Anderson-Lopez that premiered at the La Jolla Playhouse in 2015, and “Calvin Berger” by Barry Wyner.
