Immaculate Grid PGA Explained

Immaculate Golf Grid can be played by guessing players at different categories in a 3x3 cell. Players cannot be used twice and both active and inactive players can be selected. 

There are two categories in the 3x3 table; for team sports like MLB, there are names of the club in the row and a personal record such as RBI. Likewise, the column shall also feature the name of the club or some personal record. 

In the case of golf, the trophies, nationality, and tour might come into play. Additionally, personal records such as birdies, eagles, and albatrosses might be included to bring challenge and excitement to the game.

Golf Grid Trivia Samples

Golf Grid Trivia Samples are players' achievements such as the major trophy wins, OWGR, and team wins in Ryder Cup. These accomplishments of the golfers should be filled in the 3x3 table that meets its criteria. 

It is a simple trivia game where a golfer should meet two categories to be selected as the correct answer. Also, even if it is wrong the total of nine guesses shall decrease with each guess. 

Likewise, players can score higher points, if they make the most difficult choices while making the guess, such as players who have won the event only once in their golfing career.

Set of Rules for Golf Trivia

  • The quiz can be played timed but contestant guesses shall be over after nine times
  • Both past and present players can be included in the grid
  • In the case of team play like Ryder Cup, injured teammates throughout the competition will not be ruled correct thus some matches should be played by the player.
