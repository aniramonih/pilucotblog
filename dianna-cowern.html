Was Terry Davis, Dianna Cowern's Boyfriend? Know Dianna's Relationship Status and YouTube Channel

Dianna Cowern is an American YouTuber who gained fame by explaining Physics. In this age of the internet, any person having talent can use YouTube platforms and become phenomenal. She uploads videos of various physical phenomena explaining their cause to her audience. 

Dianna was born on May 4, 1989, as Dianna Leilani Cowern in Kauai, Hawaii, U.S. She graduated from Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 2011, where she studied Physics. Know in the internet world as Physics Girl, has a huge demographic of the audience. Cowern is a true example of beauty with brains, and in this article, let's explore who's she dating?

Her Self-Made Career

Dianna works as a science communicator on her channel Physics Girl. She was inspired by the likes of Neil deGrasse Tyson and his work as a science communicator. Dianna started her channel in 2011 after her graduation from the University. 

She posted her explanation videos since then but it was in 2014 Cowern rose to prominence. In 2014, Cowern won actor Alan Alda's "flame challenge". It is a competition where scientists are asked to answer basic questions about the satisfaction of school children. After her victory, PBS Digital Studio offered to sponsor her YouTube channel.

Apart from science videos she also uploads Skit videos on her channel. Dianna uploads anywhere between 1 to 4 videos a month.

Outside of YouTube

Cowern after her graduation was a research fellow at the Harvard Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics. She also was the outreach coordinator at the University of California at San Diego's Center for Astrophysics and Space Sciences. 

The YouTuber always wanted to make science more accessible to girls. When she started making her initial science videos she was still working at General Electric. Cowern is an active figure and discussed science on national media, like U.S News & World Report, and APS News. 

She also featured in Slate, Scientific American and Huffington Post blogs. Cowern has also hosted the 'Google Science Fair'. She, furthermore, was listed in Forbes 30 under 30 in the education category in 2018. Recently, she is joining other YouTuber's at World Economic Forum 2020 in Mumbai hosted by Bhuvan Bam.
