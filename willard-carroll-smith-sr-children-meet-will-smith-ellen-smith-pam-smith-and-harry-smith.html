Meet Will Smith, Ellen Smith, Pam Smith, and Harry Smith

Willard Carroll Smith Sr, father of the famous Will Smith was a refrigeration engineer and U.S, air force veteran.

He died on November 7,2016 of cancer. Smith Sr. was married to Caroline Bright till 2000.

He lived at Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

Willard Carroll Smith Sr. children: Meet Will Smith, Ellen Smith, Pam Smith, and Harry Smith

Will Smith popularly called, Fresh Prince is the second child of Smith Sr.

Will Smith is an American actor, rapper and film producer. He was born on September 25,1968 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

He started rapping at the age of 12. Will Smith attended Our Lady of Lourdes elementary school and proceeded to Overbrook high school.

Will Smith got married to Sheree Zampino in 1992. They have a son together, his name is Willard Carroll ‘Trey’ Smith III. He divorced Sheree in 1995.

In 1997, he married Jada Koren Pinkett an actress. Their children are Jaden Christopher Syre Smith and Willow Camill Reign Smith.

Will Smith is on Instagram @willsmith

Ellen Smith and Harry Smith are twins. They were born in 1971. Ellen is on Instagram @iame.smith.

Harry is known to be an entrepreneur. He is married to Ananda Lewis. They have two children.

Pamela is the eldest of all the siblings. She is known to be an actress. She was born in 1964.
