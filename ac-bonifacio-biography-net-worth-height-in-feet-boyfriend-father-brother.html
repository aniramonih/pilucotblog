AC Bonifacio Biography; Net Worth, Height In Feet, Boyfriend, Father, Brother

AC Bonifacio is a renowned Filipina-Canadian dancer, singer, video blogger and actress. Andree Camille “AC” Lapidario Bonifacio was born on December 13, 2002, in Vancouver, British Columbia. Her father is Arnold Bonifacio while her mother’s name is Cherry Lapidario. Not much is known regarding her childhood and family background.

AC Bonifacio is of Filipino descent and was raised alongside a brother Ace Bonifacio in Vancouver. With regards to her education, AC went to Queensborough Middle School in New Westminster, Canada.

AC Bonifacio rose into the limelight as the first-ever grand champion of Filipino TV dance competition Dance Kids along with Lucky Ancheta in 2016. She is presently a Hall of Famer in Los Angeles, California, United States.
