Who Are Alexee Trevizos Parents?

A young woman (19 years old) has been charged with the murder of her newborn son inside a hospital bathroom in Artesia. The tragic incident occurred when she allegedly placed her baby in a hospital trash bag. Although the young woman initially claimed that the child was stillborn, the autopsy report denied her statement.

Evidence emerged indicating that the young woman had indeed been pregnant, prompting her to retreat to the bathroom. Hospital staff became concerned and urged her to open the door, eventually gaining access. Inside, they discovered blood and provided her with medical assistance. During cleanup, a hospital team member noticed that the trash bag felt unusually heavy. Upon further inspection, a newborn baby boy, showing no signs of life, was found inside the bag.

On May 10, Trevizo was charged and arrested for first-degree murder. However, she has since been released from jail and will be allowed to complete the school year without being subjected to an ankle monitor or house arrest while awaiting trial.

Who Are Alexee Trevizo Parents?

The names of Alexee Trevizo’s parents have not been disclosed. However, it has been reported that Trevizo’s mother had a confrontation with her daughter at the hospital.

In a recorded video, a doctor, wearing a white lab coat, can be seen discussing the situation with a police officer, Alexee Trevizo, and her mother. Trevizo is lying on a hospital bed while her mother sits by her feet. Tearfully, Trevizo admits, “It came out of me, I put it in the bag. In the trash bag. I’m sorry, mom.”
