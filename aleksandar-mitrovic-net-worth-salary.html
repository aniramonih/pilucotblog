Aleksandar Mitrovi Net Worth &amp; Salary

On September 16, 1994, Aleksandar Mitrovic, a professional football player, was born in Serbia. He is a forward for the Premier League club Fulham as well as the Serbian national team.

Mitrovic started his football career at Partizan, where he went through the club’s development system. During the 2011-2012 season, he made his senior debut with their affiliated team Teleoptik, scoring seven goals in 25 league appearances.

On June 27, 2012, Mitrovic and Partizan signed to a four-year professional contract. In his official club debut, he scored nine minutes after being replaced in a Champions League qualifying game against Valletta.

He also scored his first league goal against Jagodina at home. Despite being one of the team’s younger members, Mitrovic concluded his debut season as Partizan’s top scorer overall, with 15 goals in 36 games.

According to the Serbian sports website Mozzart Sport, he was ranked third among the top 25 players in the Jelen SuperLiga, and he was a part of the league’s Team of the Season.
