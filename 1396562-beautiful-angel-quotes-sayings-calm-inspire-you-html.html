Beautiful angel quotes and sayings to calm and inspire you

God blessed us with his angels so that they could inspire and help us. They respond to our needs and help us in different ways. Here are some of the beautiful angel quotes that will inspire and calm you.

In our everyday life, we encounter difficulties that sometimes discourage us. Angels are always around us to uplift us even though we may not realize it. Below are some o the most inspiring angel sayings that will encourage you.

Inspiring quotes about angels

These beautiful angels quotes will help you aspire to become the best version of yourself and motivated you to do better.

  • Angels descending, bring from above, echoes of mercy, whispers of love. -Fanny J. Crosby
  • Be an Angel to someone else whenever you can, as a way of thanking God for the help your Angel has given You. - Eileen Elias Freeman

  • Angels encourage us by guiding us onto a path that will lead to happiness and hope. -Andy Lakey

  • Sometimes we are like an angel with one wing, and we necessarily need the other wing of a similar angel to keep flying. -M. K. Soni
  • Respect is more than politeness. Respect reminds us of the tender, loving reverence of a child, of a disciple, or of an angel. -Lawrence G. Lovasik
