Who is Rene Higuita:

Rene Higuita is a famous Soccer Player. He was born on August 27, 1966 and his birthplace is Medellín, Colombia. Rene is also well known as, Former Colombian goalkeeper who pioneered a daring playing style that involved leaving the goal to pass, dribble, and even shoot. He is the fourth all-time leading goal-scorer for keepers with 41 career goals.

Rene is originated from Colombia. Cameroon striker Roger Milla scored on him to knock Colombia out of the 1990 World Cup.
