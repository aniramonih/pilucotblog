A quick stroll at Rob Dyrdek bio:

This American entrepreneur, actor, producer, reality TV personality, and former skateboarder was born in Kettering, Ohio, on June 28th, 1974. He completed his high school at Fairmont High School. Dyrdek knew he wanted to be a professional Skateboarder when he dreamt of doing tricks in school during his classes. 

He earned a scholarship to represent Surf Ohio when he was just 11 years old. At the age of 12, he officially started skating. He started competing, which impressed the Gordon and Smith Company. The company ended up sponsoring him for various events. It wasn’t a wrong decision for them since Rob won the National Skateboard Association North Central District Competition, coming in first in 1989 when he was 15. 

He dropped out of High school and decided to give his professional life a shot. He then moved to Southern California. He officially went pro in 1991 when he competed at the Skateboard World cup held in Germany. He won fifth place in the global competition. 

He gave the acting profession a try when he bagged his reality show ‘Rob and Big’ in November 2006. This made him a huge celebrity and opened up the path of reality TV for him. He starred in reality shows like ‘Rob Dyrdek’s Fantasy Factory’ (2009-2015), ‘Ridiculousness’ (2015- present), and ‘Wild Grinders’ (2012-2015).
