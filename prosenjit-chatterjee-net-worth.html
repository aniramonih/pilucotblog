What is Prasenjit Chatterjee's Net Worth?

Prasenjit Chatterjee is an Indian actor and producer who has a net worth of $30 million. Prasenjit Chatterjee was born in Kolkata, India in September 1962. He works in Bengali and Hindi cinema and is referred to as "Bumba Da" by his fans. Chatterjee first became known for his role in the 2003 film Chokher Bali.

He won the National Award Silver Lotus Award for his role in Dosar in 2007. Chatterjee has more than 300 acting credits to his name. He also serves as a Chief Mentor at the International Institute of Photography in Kolkata. He has won several awards for Best Actor including BFJA Awards, Anandalok Awards, Kalakar Awards, National Film Awards, ABP Ananda Awards, Stardust Awards, and Filmfare Awards East.
