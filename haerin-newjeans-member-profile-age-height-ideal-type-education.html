Haerin (NewJeans Member) Profile, Age, Height, Ideal Type, Education

Haerin is a South Korean singer who is best known as the member of Korean girl group, ‘NewJeans’ formed by ADOR. The group is composed of five members – Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin and Hyein. At the age of 13, she started out as a trainee and debuted with NewJeans at the age of 16. NewJeans released their self-titled debut EP in August 2022. More than 1.2 million followers follow her Instagram account, newjeans_official, which features her and her fellow NewJeans members.

Who Is Haerin | Age, Height, Education

Haerin age is 16 years old, as of 2023. She was born on May 15, 2006 in in Gimcheon, Gyeongsangbuk-do, South Korea. She holds Korean nationality and belongs to mixed race. She has a younger sister, born in 2009. As per her educational qualifications, during her school years, Haerin attended Kwiin Elementary School, Seoul Boramae Elementary School, and Pyeongchan Middle School. During her school years, she was a member of the broadcasting club.

Haerin height is 5 feet 4 inches and body weight is 50 Kg. She has blonde hair and has light brown eyes. Her body measurements are 34-28-42 inches.
