This extraordinary feat, dubbed the “Miracle on the Hudson,” not only saved the lives of all 155 people on board but also catapulted Captain Sully to international fame. As we approach 2024, many are curious about the net worth of this heroic pilot and how his life has unfolded since that fateful day.
