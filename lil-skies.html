American Rapper, Lil Skies Is the Father of a Son And Has Hefty Sum to His Name

If You Love listening to Rap Music, especially from the American Musician then, Lil Skies must not be a new name to you. Lil Skies started his singing career in August 2015 with a music video, Da Sauce. 

Lil Skies was born as Kimetrius Christopher Foose on August 4, 1998, in Chambersburg, Pennsylvania. He is born among the three children born to Michael Burton, Jr. and his former wife, Shelby. Lil graduated from Waynesboro Area Senior High School in 2016 and joined Shippensburg University of Pennsylvania. His parents separated soon after his birth.

How Rich is Lil Skies?

Lil Skies comes from a musical family background. Lil's father was a struggling musician. He used to perform under the name Dark Skies. Lil is living a happy life with his successful journey in music. 

Lil Skies has an estimated net worth of $3 Million. He has accumulated most of his assets through his Musical career. He first entered a Music Recording Studio at the age of four. He admires Lil Wayne being the biggest music Inspiration.

Musical Journey of Lil Skies

Skies released his first major label mix tape, Life of Dark Rose, in January 2018. This album reached a peak of number 10 on the Billboard 200 Chart. In addition, His later songs have been listed in the Billboard Hot 100. 

In Early 2019, Lil released his second album, Shelby, and became a instant hit with his listeners. In Mid 2019, He collaborated with another famous rapper, Machine Gun Kelly, for the song, Burning Memories.
