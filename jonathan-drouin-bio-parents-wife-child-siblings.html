Jonathan Drouin Bio, Parents, Wife, Children, Siblings, Net Worth

Jonathan Drouin Bio, Parents, Wife, Children, Siblings, Net Worth: Jonathan Drouin is a Canadian professional ice hockey player born on March 28, 1995.

He developed a passion for playing ice hockey at a younger age, has been consistent throughout his career, and has risen through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after ice hockey players.

At the time of this writing (July 2, 2023), Drouin is a professional ice hockey left winger for the Colorado Avalanche of the National Hockey League (NHL).

Growing up, he played in the 2008 Quebec Internati onal Pee-Wee Hockey Tournament with the Northern Selects minor ice hockey team.

Later, Drouin was drafted second overall by the Halifax Mooseheads in the 2011 Quebec Major Junior Hockey League (QMJHL) Entry Draft

Following a stellar 2012–13 season with Halifax Mooseheads, he was selected by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the first round, third overall, of the 2013 NHL Entry Draft.

In June 2017, he was traded to the Montreal Canadiens. As a restricted free agent, he was then signed to a six-year, $33 million contract with the Canadiens.

After playing a total of six seasons for the Montreal Canadiens, his six-year contract ended in June 2023 hence becoming an unrestricted free agent from July 1.

In July 2023, Jonathan Drouin hit major headlines after signing a one-year, $825,000 contract with the Colorado Avalanche as an unrestricted free agent from the Canadiens.

Internationally, Drouin won a gold medal at the 2012 Ivan Hlinka Memorial Tournament as a member of Canada’s under-18 team.

Later, he represented Canada’s junior team at the 2013 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships in Ufa, Russia, where Canada finished 4th
