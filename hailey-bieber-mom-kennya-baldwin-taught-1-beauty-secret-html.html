Hailey Bieber's Mom, Kennya Baldwin, Taught Her 1 Beauty Secret

Hailey Bieber is best known for her fashion modeling career and marriage to pop star Justin Bieber. But she is also the youngest daughter of actor Stephen Baldwin and his wife, Kennya Baldwin.

Hailey Bieber comes from a close, supportive family, so it’s no wonder she turns to her parents for advice about love, marriage, and even beauty products.

Kennya Baldwin is a graphic designer and mother of 2

53-year-old Kennya Baldwin is the mother of two daughters, including Hailey. She is married to actor Stephen Baldwin and has a career in graphic design. She’s also the daughter of the world-renowned Brazilian pianist Eumir Deodato.

Kennya Baldwin plays a huge role in her daughter’s life, even now as Bieber herself is a wife. When Justin proposed to Hailey in 2018, the first thing she did was talk to her parents about it.

“In my heart, I knew that’s what I wanted to do,” she said of saying yes to marrying Justin in the YouTube docuseries Justin Bieber: Seasons. But she wanted to see if her parents would “stop” her “from doing something crazy.”

In fact, her father, Stephen, had introduced his daughter to her future husband years earlier when she was just a child.

And even when times get tough, her mother is always there for her.

“I remember I called her a few different times,” Bieber told the podcast In Good Faith With Chelsea & Judah Smith about a breaking point in her young marriage. “One particular time when we were in Brooklyn, and I was calling her, I was crying, and I was like, ‘I just can’t do it. There’s no way that I’m going to be able to do this if it’s going to be like this forever.

“And I just remember she was so calm on the phone. And she was like, ‘It’s going to pass, and you’re going to be fine, and he’s going to be healthy, and we’re here for you.'”

Kennya Baldwin taught Hailey Bieber an important beauty tip

When someone is deep in the modeling and beauty industries, it’s important to know the best beauty secrets to keep a flawless complexion. Hailey Bieber relies on one specific beauty tip from her mother that has helped the model maintain her radiant skin. The secret? Using organic, clean beauty products.

“My mother is Brazilian, and I grew up in a house where she and my grandmother only used organic products, from hair dyes to cleaning products,” Bieber told Vogue India. “I’ve been surrounded by natural and clean beauty my whole life.”

According to CNN, the clean beauty trend has taken off, with many major brands offering clean and green beauty products that are free from toxins and other potentially harmful ingredients.

Hailey Bieber’s modeling career has taken off

With her beautiful skin, it’s no wonder Hailey Bieber is a successful model. She has been modeling since 2014, when she signed with Ford Models. Her career began with photos in print magazines such as Tatler, Love, and V. Her first runway show was in October 2014 for Topshop.

Soon, Bieber was landing modeling jobs with prominent publications such as Vogue and Teen Vogue. She has also walked the runway for designers Tommy Hilfiger, Philipp Plein, and Ralph Lauren. In addition, Bieber has participated in many ad campaigns for brands and events such as Coachella. And in 2019, she became the face of Levi’s.

Not only does she model, but Bieber has also taken the next step in her career by creating capsule collections with The Daily Edited and Public Desire. On top of that, she’s working on a makeup collection called Rhode (her middle name). According to MSN, the product line will drop this summer.
