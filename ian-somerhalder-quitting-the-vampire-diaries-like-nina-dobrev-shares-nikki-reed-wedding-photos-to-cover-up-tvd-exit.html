Ian Somerhalder Quitting The Vampire Diaries Like Nina Dobrev: Shares Nikki Reed Wedding Photo

Ian Somerhalder and Nikki Reed have shared their wedding pictures covering Instagram hints that the TVD star will exit ‘The Vampire Diaries.’ The images of Somerhalder carrying Reed down cobblestone path join photos of Ian and Nikki’s wedding rings, reception cake, and Nikki Reed’s bouquet of flowers. If all the speculation of Ian Somerhalder leaving ‘The Vampire Diaries’ is true, it’s going to take more than a few wedding photos to distract TVD fans from Damon Salvatore’s departure.

Ian and Nikki are not shy when it comes to their romance. ET bills the wedding as “fairytale” which fits in with the supernatural theme of Somerhalder’s CW show, but there isn’t much of magical enchantment of their story. The two met courtesy of Ian’s now former costar and then girlfriend, Nina Dobrev, who Ian had been dating since 2011. Nikki was married to American Idol alum, Paul McDonald, but that didn’t stop a friendship from growing into romance. Somerhalder and Dobrev split after 3 years of dating, and Ian and Nikki announced their engagement just a few weeks after Nikki Reed’s divorce was finalized. Nina dismissed rumors of bad blood between herself and Nikki, but it’s evident that there were some hard feeling between the former friends.

Nina Dobrev left ‘The Vampire Diaries’ much to the dismay of fans who love the show. The next season of TVD seems to be up in the air. Producers and the cast have teased the possible return of Elena, but Nina has shut down the rumors insisting she is done with the role during her speech at the 2015 Teen Choice Awards.

As CDL covered, Ian has been posting some cryptic messages via his Instagram account hinting at his possible departure from ‘The Vampire Diaries’ as he is looking happily toward “the future.” In an interview with TV Guide, Ian doesn’t seem to have too much confidence in recreating the chemistry Nina brought as Elena saying, “Elena even told him, ‘Go live your life!’ — i.e. get into some panties, do whatever you need to do, and enjoy it. I don’t think he’s quite there, [but] any woman coming into the fray has to be so unbelievably bad ass that the audience loves this person first because it’ll be trouble. The fandom is extremely opinionated. They’re very set on this Elena-Damon dynamic, so I wish all the best of luck in the world to whoever that is.”
