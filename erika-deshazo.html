New York's Former Star Dating?

Erika DeShazo is an American reality star who first gained stardom as the supporting cast member of the VH1 reality series Love & Hip Hop: New York in the 6th season. It premiered on March 6, 2011. 

Asides from her reality career, she works as a real estate developer in New York and a fashion designer. Find out more interesting details about her in the following sections.

Is Erika DeShazo Single?

The 37 years old Erika DeShazo is probably a single lady. She mostly keeps her personal or love details ut of the media eyes at the moment. Hence, she is not in a romantic relationship with anyone.

However, DeShazo is known as the ex-girlfriend of Love & Hip Hop costar, Mendeecees Harris, 42. He is a TV personality and real estate, entrepreneur. They started dating in 2003 after first meeting on the show, Love & Hip Hop. Together, they welcomed a son, Aasim. 

However, the couple split in 2011 and she claimed that she found Harris dating another woman, Yandy Smith when he was still with her. Mendeecees also dated his baby's mama Samantha Wallace and his current wife, Yandy. 

Mother Of One Child

During their togetherness, Erika and her ex-beau, Mendeecees welcomed their first child, a son, Aasim M Harris. He was born on September 7 2011, in the United States. 

Already,  Aasim becomes an ambassador and social media star with 10.1k followers on his Instagram page under the name aasimharris. Apart, he is really passionate and excited about sports and is enjoying his school days.

Moreover, Erika has three stepchildren, Mendeecees Harris, Jr., Omare Harris, and Skylar Smith-Harris. As of now, her son Aasim is very close to his half-brothers and sister. Also, he has a good relationship with his step-mother, Yandy.
