Alisan Porter Net Worth

Alisan Porter is an actress and singer born on June 20, 1981 in Worcester, MA. She is best known for her role in the 1991 film Curly Sue, as well as her appearances on The Golden Girls and The Ten Commandments: The Musical. In 2016, she won season 10 of the NBC series The Voice.

About

Actress who became known for starring in the 1991 film Curly Sue. She also appeared on The Golden Girls and in The Ten Commandments: The Musical. Also a singer, she won season 10 of the NBC series The Voice in 2016.

Before Fame

She moved to New York after high school to audition for roles and was eventually cast in the show Footloose.

Trivia

She became the youngest person to win the televised talent show Star Search at age five.

Family Life

She married Days of Our Lives actor Brian Autenrieth in 2012. They have two children together.

Associated With

She starred in The Ten Commandments: The Musical, co-starring Val Kilmer.
