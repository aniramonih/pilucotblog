Teri Polo: A Versatile and Talented Actress.

Teri Polo is a well-known actress who has appeared in various movies and TV shows.

She has also posed for Playboy magazine and been ranked among the Maxim Hot 100 Women.

Teri Polo was born on June 1, 1969, in Dover, Delaware, USA.

Currently, she is 54 Years Old.

Additionally, she is 5 feet 5 inches (165 cm) tall, and she weighs 60kgs.

Her full name is Theresa Elizabeth Polo.

Her parents are Jane (Gentry), a homemaker, and Vincent Polo, a stereo systems designer.

She has two brothers, Mike and Steve. She is of Italian, English, and German descent.

Teri Polo started studying ballet at the age of five and attended the School of American Ballet in New York when she was 13.

She dropped out of high school at 16 after winning a modeling contest for Seventeen magazine.

She moved to New York City to pursue an acting career.
