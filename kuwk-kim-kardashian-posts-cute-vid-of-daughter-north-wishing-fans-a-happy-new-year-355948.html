KUWK: Kim Kardashian Posts Cute Vid Of Daughter North Wishing Fans A Happy New Year!

Kim Kardashian took to her social media to share a super cute video of her firstborn child, daughter North, wishing her millions of followers Happy Holidays! The new year just started and the mother of four thought her fans would love to see little North spreading some cheer and positivity via her platform.

The clip was very sweet and North looked so grown up as well!

The 6 year old can be heard saying: ‘Hi everyone, have a Happy Christmas, Happy Hanukkah and a Happy New Year’s.’

The footage was shared on January 1 so it was definitely a very nice way to start the decade for some of her most dedicated fans.

And that was not all! There was also a second video in which the little girl said: ‘Happy birthday, Wendy. Happy Christmas and Happy Hanukkah.’

While it’s not clear who the Wendy she was sending her well wishes to was, the proud mother captioned the post with: ‘Look how cute I fund this in my phone! North leaving bday messages to my friends.’

North looked adorable in a black pajama and had her hair styled in slicked-back pigtails.

North being cheerful and excited for the holidays is great to see, especially since she was not really present for the family holiday photoshoot this year.

Instead, they were forced to just Photoshop her in since the child simply refused to participate and her mom could not change her mind.
