Grace West is probably one of the best contenders who could be crowned The Voice 2023. Early Life

West, the participant in season 23 of The Voice, was born on September 24, 2003, and is 20 years old as of 2023. Family

She was brought up by her parents, Daryl and Jill Rembinski, alongside her elder sister Abbey and younger brother, DJ. Grace West Career

As Grace matured, her music career blossomed, starting from singing the national anthem at her elementary school to performing it for the Toledo Mud Hens and Detroit Tigers. During her teenage years, she frequently played for several hours at gigs in Plymouth and Canton.

While exploring her interest in music, she discovered a natural talent for it and began playing her grandfather’s guitar. She made an impact on NBC’s singing series, “The Voice,” which is hosted by renowned figures such as Blake Shelton, Kelly Clarkson, Niall Horan, and Chance the Rapper. The competition comprises five distinct stages: blind auditions, battle rounds, knockouts, playoffs, and live performances.
