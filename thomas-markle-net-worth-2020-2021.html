Early Life & Biography

Thomas Markle was born on 18 July 1944 to the late Doris May Rita and the late Arnold Markle in Newport, Pennsylvania. Thomas has two brothers by the names Fredrick and Michael Markle. The Markle family is said to be the distant descendants of Mary Hussey Smith, who was the landowner of The New Hampshire Landowner and a descendant of Christopher Hussey.

Personal Life

Thomas met his first wife, Roslyn Loveless, in 1963 on a campus party at the University of Chicago where she was a student and a secretary. The couple then married in 1964 and had their first child Samantha Markle in the same year and later had a son in 1966. Their marriage ended in divorce in 1975.

Four years after his divorce to Roslyn, Thomas married his second wife, Doria Ragland, and the couple welcomed their daughter in 1981 by the name Rachel Meghan Markle who is widely known as The Duchess of Sussex. His marriage to Doria ended in divorce in 1988.

In 1990, Thomas won $750000 in The California State Lottery, but it is said he spent all the money. This is evident as of 2016; he filed for bankruptcy for having a dept of over $30000.

In 2018, it was revealed that he was not going to attend the famous wedding between his daughter and Prince Harry because he was recovering from heart surgery. It was later discovered that he was lying and that he had staged the photos and the story in return for money from the paparazzi. His daughter Samantha was then quoted saying that the pictures shared by her father were not for financial gain but as a motivation to stay healthy.

His continued paid interviews in 2018, where he was discussing his relationship with his daughter and his son in law damaged his relationship with his daughter Meghan. This is evident in 2019, where Thomas published a private letter sent to him privately by his daughter just after the wedding, explaining how much he had broken her heart.
