How Many Baby Mamas Did George Foreman Have?

The birthplace of George Foreman is Marshall, Texas. Together with his six siblings, he was raised in Houston, Texas’ Fifth Ward neighborhood. Leroy Moorehead was George’s real father, even though he was raised by J. D. Foreman, whom his mother had married when George was a little boy.

George was an upbringing that was difficult, as he acknowledged in his memoirs. At the age of 15, he left school and worked as a mugger. When he was 16, Foreman had a change of heart and persuaded his mother to enroll him in the Job Corps after watching an advertisement for the organization on television. Foreman received his GED while enrolled in the Job Corps, where he also received training as a bricklayer and carpenter.

How Many Baby Mamas Did George Foreman Have?

George Edward Foreman, a 73-year-old American author, clergyman, entrepreneur, and former professional boxer who is also renowned for the George Foreman Grill, has been married four times and has five baby mamas. Namely Mary Joan Martelly his current wife, Adrienne Calhoun (1971–1974), Cynthia Lewis (1977–1979), Sharon Goodson (1981–1982), and Andrea Skeete (1982–1985).
