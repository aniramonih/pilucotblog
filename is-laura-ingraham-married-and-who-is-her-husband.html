Is Laura Ingraham Married and Who Is Her Husband?

Laura Ingraham is not married and so does not have a husband. She is also not known to be in any relationship at the moment. However, it is speculated that she is keeping it out of the limelight.

The Fox News presenter became popular as the host of The Ingraham Angle show on Fox News. Laura Ingraham’s career has been nothing short of impressive, but despite her successful career as a TV presenter, one thing that has caught the attention of many is Laura’s marital status. The Fox News presenter has reportedly been romantically involved with some prominent men, but it is still unclear to many if she is married.

Who Is Laura Ingraham Married To?

Laura Ingraham is not currently married to anyone despite the rumors that she is. Over the course of her life, she has been romantically involved with several men, including some prominent political figures. Despite these relationships, none have resulted in her walking down the aisle. It seems that for Laura, prioritizing her career and personal pursuits takes precedence over-committing herself to marriage with another individual.

Was Laura Ingraham Ever Married?

Laura Ingraham has never been married; however, she has come close a few times. She was previously in a committed relationship with a successful businessman by the name of James V Reyes. The two were so dedicated to one another that they became engaged in the year 2005, eagerly anticipating their future together as husband and wife.

Soon after, she was diagnosed with breast cancer, which derailed their wedding plan as they, instead, focused on helping her get well. It was a particularly trying time for Laura as she had to face the harsh reality of her illness head-on. She underwent surgery on April 26th, 2005, and eventually beat the disease.

However, her relationship with Reyes did not survive because they called off the engagement. There are no details on when they eventually went their separate ways for good.

She was also allegedly engaged to her college sweetheart, Dinesh D’Souza. Details about their relationship are sketchy, but the two allegedly became close as a result of their mutual love and interest in politics. The engagement never led to an actual marriage, though.

Why Has Laura Ingraham Never Been Married?

Laura Ingraham has not made any remarks as regards why she is not married. It is speculated that her career pursuit as a successful TV presenter took precedence over everything else in her life. However, considering that she has come close once with her engagement to Reyes, it is apparent that she is not entirely averse to the institution of marriage.

Laura Ingraham and Raymond Arroyo’s Relationship

Laura Ingraham and Raymond Arroyo are not married and have never dated each other. Raymond Arroyo is an award–winning journalist, producer, and New York Times bestselling author. He is also a Fox News contributor and co-host of Fox Nation Laura & Raymond and an Editorial Advisor for FOA’s top-rated The Ingraham Angle.

It is clear that Laura Ingraham and Raymond Arroyo share a strong bond as coworkers, but their relationship is strictly platonic. While the media may speculate about their alleged romance, the truth remains that these two individuals are focused on their careers and maintaining a professional working relationship with each other.

Was Laura Ingraham Married to Kenny Kramme?

Laura Ingraham is not married to Kenny Kramme, and they have never been romantically involved. The confusion about whether or not Laura Ingraham is married to Kenny Kramme comes from the fact that she bears the same name and a similar surname to that of Kramme’s actual wife, Laura Ingle.
