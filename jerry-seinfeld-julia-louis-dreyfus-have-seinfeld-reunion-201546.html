Jerry Seinfeld, Julia Louis-Dreyfus Have Seinfeld Reunion

Jerry and Elaine, together again! Jerry Seinfeld reunited with his Seinfeld costar Julia Louis-Dreyfus on the Wednesday, June 3, episode of Comedians in Cars Getting Coffee.

Seinfeld, 61, and the Veep actress, 54, got back together on his web series, where they took a spin in a James Bond-inspired car. Naturally, the two comics had some funny back and forth banter — Housewives and Net-a-Porter slams included. "I'm so sorry," Seinfeld said to a random woman at one point. "We're doing the Seinfeld reunion and you're not in it!"

The pals ran into another star during their spin on the open road. "You don't bring any attention to yourself — at all," Hilary Swank noted to the duo as she drove off in her Audi.

"That was Hilary Swank," Louis-Dreyfus noted, "and I guess we live in Hollywood!"

Seinfeld was just as excited about the reunion as the rest of Us. "Premiere Episode ! Julia Louis-Dreyfus," he tweeted Wednesday night, previewing the episode. "Half a Seinfeld reunion. Like a dog with 2 legs."

Like Seinfeld, Louis-Dreyfus tweeted about the reunion too. "Watch what my friend @JerrySeinfeld and I did," she told her fans.

At one point during their jaunt out in Hollywood, Seinfeld noted to his former costar: "You're really making me miss the show now. The laughs we had."
