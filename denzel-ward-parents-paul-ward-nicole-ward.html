Denzel Ward parents: Paul Ward, Nicole Ward

Denzel Ward is an American football cornerback who plays for the Cleveland Browns of the National Football League. He was born on April 28, 1997.

He attended Nordonia High School in Macedonia Ohio and played college football at Ohio state.

On December 29, 2017, Ward released an official statement through his Twitter account announcing his decision to forgo his senior season and enter the 2018 National Football League Draft.

The Cleveland Browns selected Ward in the first round (fourth overall) of the 2018 NFL Draft.
