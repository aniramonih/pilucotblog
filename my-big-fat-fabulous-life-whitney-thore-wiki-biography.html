My Big Fat Fabulous Life Whitney Thore Wiki, Biography

Whitney Thore was born on April 14, 1984, to her parents Glenn and Barbara Thore. She spent her entire childhood in her hometown of Greensboro, North Carolina.

As for her educational background, Whitney was accepted into the Theatre Summer Enrichment Program at the Governor’s School of North Carolina of Meredith College in 2000. She majored in Theatre at Appalachian State University in Boone, North Carolina, after graduating from Page High School in 2002. Later, Whitney moved to South Korea after graduating from college to teach English to school children.

Picture credit: people.com

She kicked off her career after she returned to the U.S. from Korea. She became the on-air producer of the Greensboro, North Carolina, radio show Jared & Katie in the Morning on 107.5 KZL.

Whitney rose to prominence after the show created a dance video called “A Fat Girl Dancing” on 107.5 KZL’s YouTube channel on February 27, 2014. She was covered in a variety of media sites such as ABC News, NBC’s Today Show, and The Huffington Post.

TLC aired My Big Fat Fabulous Life in early January 2015, which followed Whitney’s life and her weight-loss journey. The first season aired 10 episodes, while the second season started in September 2015 on TLC. Likewise, she released her memoir, I Do It with the Lights On: And 10 More Discoveries on the Road to a Blissfully Shame-Free Life in May 2016.

For your information, Whitney has her own website and blog where she interacts with other fat acceptance activists in order to spread her message of “no body shaming.”
