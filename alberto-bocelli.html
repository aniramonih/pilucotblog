Alberto Bocelli is the brother of the famous opera singer, songwriter, and record producer, Andrea Bocelli.

His Parents and Family

He was born to parents Alessandro Bocelliand Edi Bocelli. He has only one sibling whose name is Andrea Bocelli.

Alberto is a happily married man. He and his wife Cinzia are blessed with four children, namely Alessio, Anselmo, Rachele, and Maddalena. 

His son Alessio helps him to overlook the family business of winery.

Relationship With His Brother

The two brothers share a strong bond. They have always been there for each other in all the good and bad times. 

Andrea calls him his life anchor as he takes care of the administrative aspects of his business.

Love for His Father

His father has always showered love on both the brothers. Alessandro used to take the boys for outings in his inflatable boat.

Alberto, with his brother, would follow the instructions of their father and get the engine to carry the boat to the shore. 

Although he is pursuing a scientific career, he is still looking after the family business of making wine in honor of his father. 

His love for his father is the primary reason behind running the family business of wine. His father loved making wine. 

His Brother Lost His Eyesight At an Early Age

Before the birth of Andrea, his parents were advised by the doctors to abort him.

 The doctors suggested the option of abortion because they had found that the child would be born with a disability. 

Despite knowing the fact, his parents did not follow the suggestion of the doctors. They wanted their baby to see the world. 

A few days after birth, Andrea was diagnosed with Glaucoma which left him partially blind. 

At the age of 12, he was hit by football on his head, which turned out to be the reason behind his complete blindness.
