Career

Rihanna released her first album ‘Music of the Sun’ in 2005. Since then, she has released seven more albums, including ‘Good Girl Gone Bad,’ ‘Talk That Talk,’ ‘Anti,’ ‘Loud,’ and ‘Rated R.’

Rihanna has also worked in movies like ‘Katy Perry: Part of Me,’ ‘Annie,’ ‘Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets,’ ‘Battleship,’ ‘This is the End,’ and ‘Home.’ Rihanna has worked with singers like Jay-Z, Kanye West, Nicki Minaj, Eminem, Calvin Harris, Drake, and Chris Brown.

In 2017, she also released her own Makeup line called Fenty Beauty, which has taken over the Makeup world completely. Rihanna has also collaborated with dozens of clothing brands in the past to launch her own line.

Being a beautiful woman, Rihanna is hired for several endorsement deals. She has featured in the advertisement of ‘Kodak,’ ‘Secret Body Spray,’ ‘Nivea,’ ‘Vita Coco,’ ‘Budweiser,’ ‘MAC Cosmetics,’ and ‘Optus.’

Rihanna is also an active philanthropist; in 2006, she founded the Believe Foundation that helps in the medical treatment of underprivileged children. In 2012 she founded CLF (Clara Lionel Foundation) in the memory of grandparents, which helps in creating medication and education facilities in Barbados.
