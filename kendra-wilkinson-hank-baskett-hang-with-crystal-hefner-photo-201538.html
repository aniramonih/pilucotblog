Kendra Wilkinson, Hank Baskett Hang With Crystal Hefner: Photo

Gang’s all here! Kendra Wilkinson and her husband, Hank Baskett, enjoyed a parents’ night out on Saturday, Aug. 1. The couple hung out with Wilkinson’s ex-boyfriend Hugh Hefner’s wife, Crystal, at the Playboy Mansion’s annual Midsummer Night’s Dream party in L.A.'s Holmby Hills neighborhood.

Baskett, 32, opted for a pair of blue pajamas while former Playmate Wilkinson donned a light blue nightie. Hef’s 29-year-old wife showcased her physique in a bejeweled two-piece.

The fete, which was DJ’d by Brody Jenner, had a bevy of celebs in attendance. Everyone from Justin Bieber to Amber Rose and Ryan Phillipe attended this year’s soiree. 

Wilkinson, 30, has been vocal about supporting Hefner following former Playmate Holly Madison’s shocking tell-all memoir, in which she also blasted the Kendra on Top star. On her book, Madison dubbed Wilkinson the “fakest person” she’s ever met and claimed: "Despite my attempts to befriend Kendra, she continued to push me away…Hungry for her own 'team,' Kendra desperately tried to make each new Playmate who arrived at the mansion her friend—and her friend alone."
