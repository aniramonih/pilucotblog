Becky Hill Biography:

Becky Hill is an English singer and songwriter who first came to the spotlight in 2012 when she reached the semi-final of the first series of the singing-based reality competition TV show The Voice UK. Her first EP, Losing, was released in November 2014 and her second Ep, Eko, was released in August 2017. Her first compilation album called Get to Know was released in 2019 and peaked on the Irish Albums Chart at number 14, and on the UK Albums Chart number 20. The BPI (British Recorded Music Industry) also certified the album to ‘Blue.’ Several of her singles, such as Gecko (Overdrive) (2014), Piece of Me (2016), False Alarm (2016), Back and Forth (2018), Wish You Well (2019), and Lose Control (2019), peaked in the UK charts’ ‘Top 20’ and were either BPI-certified as ‘Platinum’ or ‘Gold.’ The last of these tracks also won the ARIA (Australian Music Industry Association) ‘Platinum certification.’
