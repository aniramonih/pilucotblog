Summertime Saga Debbie: Route &amp; Walkthrough

Summertime Saga is an explicit dating simulator and visual novel style game which follows the male protagonist as he tries to find the truth behind his fathers recent death while juggling school, his finances, and trying to get a date.

We’ll walk you through Debbie’s entire route so you can get all of her endings and scens. Of course, there are a lot of spoilers, so it’s up to you whether or not you want to continue Debbie route. Summertime Saga Debbie: Route & Walkthrough.

Helping Debbie: Debbie’s route

  • Take a seat at the bar and have a drink while you listen to Debbie on the phone, who is having financial difficulties.
  • The day after, Debbie will ask for your assistance (lawn). Start by attempting to utilize the mover before purchasing a gas tank (Consum-R > Mall) . Fill up the mover and get started on your task. When you’re finished, go down to the basement and wash your clothes.
  • The following day, get down to the basement and open the door to a guy, and the day after you’ll meet Debbie and Mrs. Johnson outside.
  • The following day, meet Debbie at the entrance and repair a shower pipes. Go there, then close the valve in the basement, and return. Finally, purchase a wrench (Consum-R > Mall) and repair the pipe (shower).
  • Next day spy Debbie (shower), and the day after, go to the house front.
  • The next day go to the entrance and help Debbie with the vacuum. And the day after, in the morning, with the dishes in the kitchen. Finally, next day, with the laundry in the basement, but give her the lotion (is in her dresser), to have a massage

    • Endings: Debbie’s route

  • On the next day, open the dresser (Debbie’s room) and retrieve the boxer brief. Wait until evening to visit her at the door, watch TV with her, and have another massage.
  • The following day, take her to the entrance, then the garage, and finally to the mall. Visit Cupid (2nd floor), give her a necklace, and follow her into the fitting room where she will teach you how to kiss.
  • The Next day, go to Debbie’s bedroom, and the day after, in the kitchen ask her to teach you to kiss
  • Next day go to the shower when Debbie is washing herself
  • Next day go to the entrance, and then to the garage. Go to the dealership and make the insurance claim. It’s quite expensive, but with charisma 7 is free.
  • The Next day go to Debbie’s dresser, take the panties, and interact with the bed, when se ask you, tell her you like her, and she will give you a hand in the night.
  • Next day go outside, protect Debbie and unlock another shower sceneNext day go to the hallway, meet Debbie and follow her to her bedroom for more scenes
  • The day after spy Debbie talking with Diane in the kitchen, then try to sleep, but finally meet her in the living room for more scenes
  • Next day, after you go bed to sleep, wake up and go to the pool. Take the towel from the shower, warm up Debbie and unlock more scenes
  • Next day read the note in your computer, take the laundry basket (Debbie’s bedroom), go to the basement, and enjoy more scenes
