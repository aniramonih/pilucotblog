The Journey of a Versatile Comedian

Rahul Subramanian’s career is a fascinating blend of humor, creativity, and business acumen. Starting as a brand manager, Rahul’s transition to comedy was not just a career shift but a passion pursuit.

Alongside Kumar Varun, Rahul formed Random Chikibum, a name now synonymous with innovative comedy in India. Their victory in the inaugural YouTube Comedy Hunt was just the beginning.

Who is Rahul Subramanian?

He is a popular Indian stand-up comedian known for his interactive performances and crowd-work. Before comedy, he worked as a brand manager and pursued stand-up comedy full-time after receiving an offer from the artist management company Only Much Louder.

What are some of Rahul’s notable works?

He has been featured in Amazon Prime Video’s comedy shows like Comicstaan (2018) and Comedy Premium League (2021), and his solo special Kal Main Udega is streamed on Amazon Prime Video. He has also appeared in a short film directed by Mrinal Bahukhandi and the comedy-drama series Pushpavalli.
