How to pronounce katydid

How do you pronounce katydid in America?

What is the meaning of katydid in English?

: any of various large green American long-horned grasshoppers usually having stridulating organs on the forewings of the males that produce a loud shrill sound.

Why is a katydid Pink?

The green katydid on the left is the normal phenotype. The pink on the right is a result of erythrism – a rare genetic mutation that allows for abnormal amounts of red pigment or the absence of normal pigment – in this case greens and browns.

How do you pronounce the name Weiss?

Weiss or Weiß, also written Weis or Weisz, pronounced like “vice”, is a German and Jewish surname, meaning ‘white’ in both German and Yiddish.
