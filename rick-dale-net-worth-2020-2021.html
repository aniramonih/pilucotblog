Career

Rick started his career as a singer for a metal band. His expertise as an antiques collector and restorer was quite undermined. His abilities were discovered when History Channel hired him to be the face of their show called American Restoration. After getting such a chance, Rick refused to look behind. He managed to amass a significant following due to the show. Moreover, his performance in the show was praised, as well as his expert opinion, which was deemed to be extremely valuable to this line of work.

He was the star of American Restoration for about six seasons, from the very first season of the show till its sixth season. After this, Rick Dale and his family were released by the upper echelons of History Channel since they decided that the show needed a revamp. After this, a new format was given to the show, along with an original cast. In real life, Rick Dale is the proud owner of an antique restoration facility called Rick Restorations, which is in the city of Las Vegas. This is the institution which was prominently featured in the show American Restorations.

From the end of October 2010, his show started being shown on History Channel, which provided a massive increment to the popularity of Rick Dale. Dale, by himself, was garnering quite a bit of reputation on his own. However, his antique restoration premises gained its real fame after being shown by the History Channel. The show was aired as a spin-off of the hugely successful show called Pawn Stars. After the sixth season, Rick and his family were replaced by other stars such as Steve Hale, Dave Walksler, Bob Halliday and a few others. The show, airing since the beginning of 2016, now has an entirely different format as well.
