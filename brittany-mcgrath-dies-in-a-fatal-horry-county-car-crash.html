Brittany McGrath Dies In a Fatal Horry County Car Crash

Brittany McGrath Dies In a Fatal Horry County Car Crash

The Horry County Coroner’s Office reports that one woman is deceased when her car on Juniper Bay Road near Conway struck a tree and a utility pole early on Saturday morning.

The woman who perished in the collision was identified as Brittany McGrath, 33, of Conway. McGrath suffered numerous catastrophic injuries and passed away at the site, according to the coroner’s office.

Horry County Fire Rescue was called to the scene at approximately 1:00 a.m. and stated that there was entrapment in the wreck. To repair the damaged pole, crews are at work.
