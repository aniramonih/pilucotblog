All About Hunter Biden’s Daughter Navy’s Mother, Lunden Roberts”

Lunden Roberts (age: 32, March 5, 1991) is a former stripper whom people often recognize as the baby mama of President Joe Biden’s son Hunter Biden. Reportedly Lunden met Biden while working as a stripper dancer at Washington’s The Mpire Club.

From Roberts, Hunter has a daughter – Navy Joan Roberts. Initially, Hunter Biden denied being the father of Navy Joan Roberts. A DNA test was conducted to settle the matter, confirming Hunter’s paternity.

The test results revealed that Hunter was indeed the child’s father, and he subsequently acknowledged his relationship with Navy.

According to sources, in June 2023, Hunter and Lunden settled the child support case. As per the settlement, Lunden will receive $5,000 monthly from Hunter as child support instead of $20,000.

Lunden, meanwhile, dropped an earlier attempt to change the Navy’s last name to “Biden,” according to the court filing. In July 2023, Joe Biden, in a speech, openly acknowledged the Navy as his seventh grandchild. He said:

Our son Hunter and Navy’s mother, Lunden, are working together to foster a relationship that is in the best interests of their daughter, preserving her privacy as much as possible going forward. This is not a political issue, it’s a family matter. Jill and I only want what is best for all of our grandchildren, including Navy.

Hunter Settled Child Support Case

The confirmation of paternity led to a prolonged legal battle over child support between Lunden Roberts and Hunter Biden. The dispute spanned several years, and in November 2019, the court ruled in Lunden’s favor, ordering Hunter to pay child support.

After an extended legal process, Hunter Biden and Lunden Roberts settled in June 2023. As part of the settlement, Hunter agreed to pay Lunden $5,000 monthly in child support, a reduced amount from the initial $20,000.

Additionally, some of Hunter’s paintings were included in the settlement as part of the support for their daughter.

During the legal proceedings, Lunden Roberts attempted to change Navy’s last name to “Biden,” but she later dropped this effort. The name change request was no longer pursued following the settlement agreement.

How Did Lunden Roberts Meet Hunter Biden?

Lunden Roberts first crossed paths with Hunter Biden during her time as a stripper at Washington’s The Mpire Club. The exact details of how their relationship developed remain private, but it is known that they were together in 2017.

This relationship resulted in the birth of their daughter, Navy Joan Roberts, on August 28, 2018. However, Hunter initially denied the paternity and demanded a DNA test, ultimately confirming his relationship with Navy.
