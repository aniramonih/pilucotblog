Meet Hayden Hopkins, the stunning Cirque Du Soleil dancer spotted with Raiders owner Mark Davis at N

HAYDEN Hopkins was the star attraction at the NFL game between Raiders and Chargers.

The stunning blonde has been spotted sitting next to Las Vegas owner Mark Davis on December 4.

TV cameras picked up the pair watching the contest at Allegiant Stadium, which the Raiders eventually won 27-20.

The internet was ablaze trying to find out the mystery woman's identity after her brief but unforgettable appearance.

Fans quickly tracked her down, with Davis' guest of honor reportedly confirmed as Cirque Du Soleil performer Hopkins.

According to the biography on her official website, 25-year-old Hayden grew up in Seattle, Washington.

Dancing from a young age, she won numerous national titles and started assisting professional choreographers in her early teens.

By the time she was 19, she'd been on the cover of Dance Spirit and Dance Informa magazine.

Hopkins relocated to New York City to start her own dancewear label HHxx and pursue a degree.

The talented dancer has also performed alongside Jason Derulo and appeared on Good Morning America.

She made the switch to Las Vegas five years ago and made her Cirque Du Soleil debut in April 2017.

Hayden has built up a massive 211k followers on Instagram and often updates her fans with glamorous snaps.

Her TikTok page also features videos of Hayden showing off her incredible dance skills on the set of the Vegas show Mystere.

She shared an Instagram Story from the stands on Sunday, showing off her VIP seats alongside Davis with a TV built in the front.

And she confirmed that she and Davis were just neighborly friends after being asked about the experience on Instagram.

She replied to a fan: "Haha MD [Mark Davies] is a legend!

"He's my neighbor/friend. Fun game."

Las Vegas are 5-7 after the Chargers win but have struggled in 2022 despite bringing in standout receiver Davante Adams in the off-season.

Davis has a reported net worth of $1.9 billion, while the Raiders are supposedly worth $5.1 billion.
