James Brown, a native of the United States, was a musician, singer, dancer, record producer, and bandleader. He died on December 25, 2006. His status as the “Godfather of Soul” was widely regarded.

In his 73rd year, James Brown passed away.

The American musician James Brown was born on May 3, 1933, in Barnwell, South Carolina. He was raised by parents Joseph James and Susie Brown. James Brown allegedly passed suddenly on December 25, 2006, as a result of a heart attack and fluid in his lungs.

James Brown children: Meet Teddy Brown, James Joseph Brown II, Venisha Brown, LaRhonda Pettit, Daryl Brown, Deanna Brown Thomas, Lisa Brown, Larry Brown, Terry Brown

At the time of his passing, James Brown was wed to Tomi Rae Hynie. James Brown had previously been to Deidre Jenkins, Velma Warren, and Adrienne Rodriguez separately.

James Brown had nine kids before he passed away. Terry, Larry, Daryl, James Joseph Brown II, Lisa, Dr. Yamma Noyola Brown Lumar, Deanna, and Venisha Brown are his children.
