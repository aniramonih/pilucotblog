WHO IS SHAKA KHAN? | BIOGRAPHY AND Lifestyle OF CHAKA KHAN

Chaka Khan is an American singer and songwriter famous for her songs ‘I Feel For You’, ‘I’m Every Woman’ and ‘Ain’t Nobody’ and former member of the band Rufus. The Grammy Award winner has achieved a lot of success thanks to the group Rufus with songs like ‘Sweet Thing’, ‘Tell Me Something Good’ and ‘Hollywood’. Her birth name is Yvette Marie Stevens.

Chaka is known for her powerful voice, a large volume of curly hair and her charming stage presence. She first gained recognition in the 1970s. At the age of 11, Chaka and her sister Taka Doom (Yvonne Stevens) formed their first band, the Crystalettes. Khan and her sister Taka Doom later got engaged in Afro-Arts theater and formed another musical group, the Shades of Black. Chaka was inspired by music heroine Billie Holiday and Gladys Knight.

CHAKA KHAN AGE AND BIRTHDAY

CHAKA KHAN RUFUS

Chaka joined the band Rufus in 1970 which had a strong R&B and funk sound and gave the world his powerful voice with their debut album, Rufus, when the band released ‘Feel Good’ and ‘Whoever’s Thrilling You’ in 1973.

The Rufus group released their second album, Rags to Rufus, in 1974 with great hits such as “Tell Me Something Good”. As they gained fame they renamed the group, Rufus with Chaka Khan and later changed to Rufus & Chaka Khan. Chaka helped the band write the single ‘Sweet Thing’ which was released in 1975. The band went on to release hits like ‘Hollywood’, ‘Do You Love What You Feel’ and ‘Ain’t Nobody’.

CHAKA KHAN I FEEL FOR YOU

Chaka released the album, I Feel for You in 1984, which covered a single originally written and recorded by Prince in 1979. The track incorporated both rap and R&B and electronic dance music. I Feel for You won a Chaka Grammy Award in 1984. The album had other hits such as ‘This Is My Night’ and ‘Through the Fire’.

CHAKA KHAN IS NOBODY

In 183, the band Rufus released a live album, Stompin’ at the Savoy – Live, which featured the hit single “Ain’t Nobody” which was Rufus’ last hit reaching number 22 on the Billboard Hot 100 and number one on the Chart. Hot R&B. Ain’t Nobody also reached the top 10 in the UK. After the release of this live album, Stompin’ at the Savoy – Live, the band Rufus broke up for good.

NET WORTH OF CHAKA KHAN

Chaka has enjoyed a long career in the music industry that spans over five decades. Thanks to the proceeds of her work as a singer and songwriter, Chaka was able to accumulate a modest fortune. Chaka Khan is estimated to have a net worth of around $30 million.
