Early Life

Bob Newhart was born in 1929 on 5th September to his late parents George David Newhart and Julia Pauline. His birth name is George Robert Newhart, while his birthplace was Oak Park, Illinois. His mother was a housewife. On the other hand, his father was one of the owners of a business dealing with heating-supply and plumbing. Newhart was born together with her three sisters, namely Virginia, Pauline, and Mary Joan.

He studied in Roman Catholic schools, such as St. Ignatius College Prep. He graduated from the latter in 1947. Bob then joined the Loyola University of Chicago. He graduated with a bachelor’s degree in business management in 1952. Despite attending the Loyola University Chicago School of Law, he did not complete his law studies after an incident during his internship.

Career

After his friend withdrew, Newhart continued to do it alone.

Thanks to his album titled The Button-Down Mind of Bob Newhart caught the attention of many people. It was position 20 among the bestselling albums. The next one was another hit called Button Mind Strikes Back. He has two successful sitcoms as well, namely Newhart and The Bob Newhart Show.
