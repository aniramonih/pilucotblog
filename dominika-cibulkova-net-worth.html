What is Dominika Cibulkova's Net Worth?

Dominika Cibulkova is a Slovakian professional tennis player who has a net worth of $8 million. Dominika Cibulkova was born in Bratislava, Czechoslovakia in May 1989. She started playing tennis at eight years old and turned professional in 2004. Cibulkova has at least reached the quarterfinals of all four Grand Slam tournaments and has won over $6.5 million in career prize money. Her highest singles ranking came at #10 in March 2014.

Cibulkova reached the finals of the Australian Open in 2014 and the semifinals of the French Open in 2009. She has won four WTA titles and two ITF championships. She has not won a doubles title but reached the quarterfinals of the US Open in 2008. Cibulkova won the Hopman Cup in 2009 and reached the semifinals of the Fed Cup in 2013. She was defeated by Li Na at the 2014 Australian Open in straight sets.
