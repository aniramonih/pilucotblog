    Early Life

    Maya Rudolph was born Maya Khabira Rudolph on July 27, 1972, in Gainesville, Florida. Her father is composer Richard Rudolph, and her mother is singer-songwriter Minnie Riperton, who sadly died of breast cancer when Maya was just 6 years old. Maya has a brother named Marc, and when they were very young, the family moved to Los Angeles, California. Maya attended the Crossroads School in Santa Monica, where she was friends with future stars Jack Black and Gwyneth Paltrow. After graduating in 1990, she enrolled at the University of California, Santa Cruz, earning a Bachelor of Arts degree in photography in 1995.
