Jessica Forkum: Who Is Sonny Grays Wife?

Sonny Gray is an American professional baseball pitcher for the Minnesota Twins of Major League Baseball.

Gray made his MLB debut in 2013. He won the American League Pitcher of the Month Award twice in 2014 and was named an MLB All-Star in 2015.

The Yankees acquired Gray in 2017 and traded him to the Reds before the 2019 season.

On March 13, 2022, the Cincinnati Reds traded pitcher Gray and minor league right-hander, Francis Peguero, to the Minnesota Twins in exchange for Chase Petty.
