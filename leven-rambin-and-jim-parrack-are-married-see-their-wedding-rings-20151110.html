Leven Rambin and Jim Parrack Are Married: See Their Wedding Pics!

Congratulations, Leven Rambin and Jim Parrack! The couple tied the knot on Saturday, Oct. 10, in Buda, Texas!

The True Detective actress, 25, shared a photo on Instagram on Sunday, Oct. 11, showing off their wedding rings.

“#married @parrack120,” she simply captioned the photo. The sweet pic shows the couples’ hands clasped, with their wedding rings prominently displayed.

Leading up to the wedding, both the actress and the True Blood star shared photos on Instagram teasing the big day.

“My soon to be wife and forever #wcw @levenrambin right where we belong,” the actor, 34, wrote of a photo he shared on Wednesday, Sept. 30, which showed the couple embracing with a smile.

And on the day before the wedding, Rambin shared two cute pics.

“Let’s do this @parrack120,” she captioned one photo that showed her twirling in a white and pink dress. The other photo featured her dog wearing a sparkly collar. “Sooks is getting allll ready for the big day with a 70 karat bracelet by @tacoriofficial,” Rambin wrote.

Parrack’s former True Blood costar and on-screen vampire bride, Deborah Ann Woll, offered her best wishes for the couple. On Saturday, Oct. 10, she tweeted, “Congratulations to @parrack120 and @levenrambin on their wedding today!!! All the best! #trueblood.”
