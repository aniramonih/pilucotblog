Marcus Stoinis Biography

Marcus Stoinis, age 31, was born on August 16th, 1989 in Perth, Western Australia. Marcus Stoinis’ mother’s name is unknown, and his father is Chris Stoinis. Marcus Stoinis’ sister is named Natasa.

Marcus Stoinis is an all-around Australian player who bats right-handed and is mostly a medium pacer. In 2008, Stoinis traveled to Malaysia with the Australian Under-19 World Cup team, which included featured talents like Phil Hughes, Josh Hazlewood, James Pattinson, Steven Smith, and James Faulkner. At the age of 19, Stoinis earned his Western Australian debut in January 2009. He chose to go to Victoria for the 2012–13 season, although he was unable to establish himself as a consistent starter.
