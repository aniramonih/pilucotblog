Gabrielle Dennis has also starred as Tasha on The Upshaws and Tilda Johnson in the Marvel Comics-based series Luke Cage (2018). She played the role of Pippy Rosewood on the medical drama series Rosewood, Denise Roy on Blue Mountain State, and Janay on The Game.
