Adam Levine Bio, Parents, Wife, Children, Siblings, Net Worth

Adam Levine Bio, Parents, Wife, Children, Siblings, Net Worth: Born on March 18, 1979, Adam Levine, full name Adam Noah Levine is an American singer and songwriter.

He developed a love for music at a younger age and has been consistent throughout his career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after musicians.

At the time of this writing (Wednesday, May 24, 2023), Levine serves as the lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist of the pop rock band Maroon 5.

Levine kicked off his musical career in 1994 as the lead vocalist and lead guitarist of the band; Kara’s Flowers.

In February 1994, together with friends Jesse Carmichael, Mickey Madden, and Ryan Dusick formed the band; Kara’s Flower

Following the commercial failure of their only album, The Fourth World, the group; Kara’s Flowers rebranded in 2001 as Maroon 5.

The band’s debut album, Songs About Jane, went multi-platinum in the United States and since then, they have released several projects.

Levine made his acting debut as the recurring character Leo Morrison in the second season of the television series American Horror Story.

He has appeared in several other films including; Begin Again, Popstar: Never Stop Never Stopping, Fun Mom Dinner, and The Clapper, to mention a few.

Levine owns a record label, 222 Records, and a production company, 222 Productions, which produced television shows Sugar and Songland.

As part of Maroon 5, Adam Levine has received multiple accolades, including three Grammy Awards.

Adam Levine Parents

Adam Levine was born in Los Angeles, California, United States to his parents; Fred Levine (father) and Patsy Noah (mother).

His father is the founder of the retail chain M. Fredric while his mother worked as an admissions counselor.

Fred and Patsy divorced when Levine was seven and underwent therapy. Growing up, he spent weekdays with his mother and weekends with his father.

Adam Levine Wife

Adam Levine has been married to Behati Prinsloo since July 19, 2014. The couple started dating in May 2012.

Behati Prinsloo was born on May 16, 1988, in Grootfontein, Namibia to her parents; Boet Prinsloo (father) and Magda Prinsloo (mother).

Prinsloo is a Namibian model. In 2008, she became a Pink contract model and moved on to become a Victoria’s Secret Angel in 2009.

She walked in ten Victoria’s Secret Fashion Shows and opened consecutive Victoria’s Secret Fashion shows in 2014 and 2015

In 2021, Levine and Prinsloo founded a tequila company called Calirosa. The brand’s tequila bottles were made available in December of the same year.

Before the singer met Behati Prinsloo, he was in a relationship with Anne Vyalitsyna, a Russian Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue model.

Levine and Anne Vyalitsyna met in 2010 and they soon began a relationship. They ended the relationship in April 2012.

Adam Levine Children

Adam Levine has been blessed with three children. The award-winning singer shares all three children with his wife, Behati Prinsloo.

Levine and Prinsloo have two daughters, Dusty Rose (born September 21, 2016) and Gio Grace (born February 15, 2018). The couple welcomed their third child in January 2023 and are yet to share any further details.

Adam Levine Siblings

Adam Levine is not the only child of his parents; Fred Levine (father) and Patsy Noah (mother). He grew up with four other siblings; Julia Milne Levine, Michael Levine, Sam Levine, and Liza Levine.

Adam Levine Net Worth

As of May 2023, Adam Levine has an estimated net worth of about $160 million. He has made such an amount from his career as a singer and songwriter.
