Early Life & Biography

Kennedy was born to Robert. Kennedy and Ethel Kennedy on 17 January 1954 in Washington, D.C. He is the third born among the eleven children of Robert. F Kennedy.  He is the nephew of former US president John. F. Kennedy, Joseph P. Kennedy, and senator P. Kennedy.

He was 14 years old when his father was assassinated, and he learned about the shooting while at the preparatory school he studied, at that time, the Georgetown Preparatory School. He also lost his uncle John. F. Kennedy to the assassination in 1963 when he was just nine years old.

Kennedy went to High School Palfrey Street School in Massachusetts, he then joined Harvard and the London School of Economics. He graduated from Harvard in 1976 with a Bachelor of Arts History and Literature. He then went to the University of Virginia to earn a Juris Doctor and then Master of Laws from Pace University.
