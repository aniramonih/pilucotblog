Who is Mackenzie Lintz?

Mackenzie Lintz is an American television and film actress. She is well known for her appearance as a Red-head who has received various awards for her roles as Tribute Girl District 8 in the Hunger Games. 

Mackenzie was born on 22nd November 1996 in the United States of America, where another American actress Brittany Curran was born.

Being born to famous American parents Kelly Lintz, an American actress, and Marc Lintz, a real estate agent, Mackenzie is of White ethnicity and holds American nationality. 

She was raised in Alpharetta, Georgia along with her siblings, Madison Lintz, Matthew Lintz, and Macsen Lintz. Regarding her studies, Lintz attended Auburn University and studied acting and music.

At the early age of fifteen, Lintz kicked-off her acting career in the 2011 episode of Drop Dead Diva. 

Since then, she has made her appearance in various movies and series, such as Under the Dome, The hunger Games, Simon vs. The Homo Sapiens Agenda. 

In addition, she auditioned for the role of Mattie Ross in the 2010 remake movie of True Gift along with other 15,000 girls. She has also made her appearance on two seasons of the AMC's post-apocalyptic series The Walking Dead, like Sophia.

Moreover, she has also made her appearance in the television series Nashville and It's Supernatural. Lintz has also been portraying as Maddie Bosch on the Amazon series Bosch. In 2011, Mackenzie made her appearance in the independent film Newbourne County. The following year, she appeared two movies named After, and Parental Guidance.
