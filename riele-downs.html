Riele Downs is a Canadian actress who is best known for portraying roles such as Charlotte in Henry Danger and Faith Sullivan in The Best Man Holiday. She has also appeared in a variety of other TV shows and films such as Four Brothers, ReGenesis, Rookie Blue, Peep and the Big Wide World, All That, and The Adventures of Kid Danger. For her acting skills, Riele has been nominated for numerous awards such as the Kids’ Choice Awards.
