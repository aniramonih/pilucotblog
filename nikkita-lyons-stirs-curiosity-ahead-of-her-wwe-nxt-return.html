Nikkita Lyons Stirs Curiosity Ahead of Her WWE NXT Return

“The Lioness” Nikkita Lyons is set to make her return to WWE NXT, and there is anticipation surrounding her comeback. WWE has advertised her return for tonight’s episode of NXT on the USA Network.

In a digital exclusive video released by WWE to build excitement for Lyons’ return, she is seen saying, “The Lioness is back. Everyone wants to know my next move … tune in and find out.”

Fans can catch her return and see what she has in store for tonight’s post-Deadline 2023 episode of NXT from the Capitol Wrestling Center in Orlando, Florida.

WWE NXT Card for December 12th, 2023 Episode:

  • Eddy Thorpe vs. Dijak
  • Noam Dar & The Meta-Four vs. Josh Briggs, Brooks Jensen, and Fallon Henley
  • Cora Jade returns
  • Nikkita Lyons appearance

As a reminder, Ringside News will be providing live play-by-play coverage of tonight’s show over in our WWE NXT coverage hub. It promises to be a memorable episode for many reasons, so it remains to be seen how the episode will turn out.
