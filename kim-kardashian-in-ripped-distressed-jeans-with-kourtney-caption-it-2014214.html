Kim Kardashian In Ripped, Distressed Jeans With Kourtney: Caption It!

Hole-y jeans! Kim Kardashian decided to focus on her thighs instead of her infamous booty during a recent trip to Starbucks. The 33-year-old reality star stepped out with sister Kourtney Kardashian in Calabasas, Calif., on Monday, April 21, in a pair of seriously distressed denim.

The mom to baby North West paired her thigh-baring jeans with a more simple white top and white blazer, adorned with giant gold buttons. Accompanying her for the fashionable, or rather questionable Starbucks run was Kourtney, who showed off her recent tan with a white button down neatly tucked in to a black leather skirt, which also showed off her thighs through detailed cut outs.

The sisters both quenched their thirst with orders of iced tea.
