World scariest man Martyn Ford shows off bulking physique ahead of fight with 'Iranian Hulk' Sajad G

MARTYN FORD showed off his beast physique in a social media video after bulking up for his fight with 'Iranian Hulk' Sajad Gharibi.

And Ford lived up to his self-proclaimed 'World Scariest man' nickname with a jump scare towards the end of the video.

The bodybuilding giant frequently shows off shirtless, flexing his muscles and tattoos to his Instagram audience of 3.3million followers.

But he now looks bigger than ever ahead of his showdown with the even bigger Iranian Hulk.

It is Ford's first foray into the boxing ring but he warned fans his April fight at the O2 is only the beginning.

The caption on his Instagram video read: "I've been working on a strategy for April …. and this, I genuinely believe will work.

"Genuinely can't wait to let go off these hands and show the world what I've been working on. When I say, April is only the beginning, I truly mean it. I absolutely LOVE this fight game, it's trapped me, it's brought me in, and I'm Obsessed.

"As you can see, I'm not taking this lightly, I'm coming in ready to show all the doubters exactly what I've been doing and that I can stand there proud, head high knowing all I've done and am doing to achieve the unthinkable.
