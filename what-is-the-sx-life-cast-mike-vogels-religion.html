What Is The S*x/Life Cast Mike Vogels Religion?

S*x/Life cast Mike Vogel’s religion has created a lot of hype recently. 

One of the topics regarding Mike Vogel that interests netizens is his religion. Is he Christian? While there isn’t much known about his religious beliefs, it’s noted that he grew up in a family that believed in Christianity and was attending a Presbyterian Church at the time. 

Although Mike appears to practice this faith, he keeps his religious views private. The actor is married to Courtney Thorne-Smith with whom he shares three children. 

The family resides in Franklin, Tennessee and their ethnicity appears to be predominantly white European. 

His acting career began when someone noticed him at a local mall in rural Ohio when he was 17 years old working at a clothing store there, and asked him if he wanted to model or act.

Mike Vogel is set to reprise his role as Connely in the second season of the popular series, which is set to air on March 2, 2023. 

Being a devout Christian himself, Mike has openly spoken about his love for God and how it has shaped him throughout his life. 

In fact, he recently starred in The Case For Christ, a movie directed by Jon Gunn and based on Lee Strobel’s book of the same name; in this film, Mike portrays an atheist journalist who unexpectedly embraces Christianity due to his wife’s faith. 

It’s no surprise that Mike chose this role considering it mirrored his own Christian faith in real life.

Who is Mike Vogel?

Michael James Vogel was born on July 17, 1979, in Abington Township, Pennsylvania to Kathy and Jim Vogel. 

His family has German-American heritage, and both his grandfathers bravely served in World War II; one being a tank commander for the Battle of Bulge and the other functioning as a Seabee for the Navy. 

Growing up in Warminster Township with his two younger siblings Daniel Aaron and Kristin, Vogel attended William Tennent High School where he was a part of the freshman wrestling team. 

After graduation, he enrolled in Cairn University in Langhorne while also reiterating trips to New York City to fulfill acting and modeling jobs.

Mike Vogel and Courtney Vogel are a happily married couple with three beautiful children. Courtney is a former model who had worked with various companies before tying the knot with Mike. 

The two now have a family of five, as they have been blessed with three lovely kids; Cassy Renee, Charlee Bea, and Gabriel James. 

Michael is also an amazing family man who loves to spend quality time with his wife and kiddos, going on hikes and trips together. His social media profiles are evidence of that

He often shares pictures of his children online to keep fans updated on the latest family news. The Vogels currently resides in Nashville, Tennessee.

Mike Vogel can be seen reprising his role as Cooper Connelly in the second season of the Netflix drama series S*x/Life, set to premiere on March 2, 2023.
