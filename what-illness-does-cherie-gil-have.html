What Illness Does Cherie Gil Have: Did Actress Death Cause By Cancer? Family Details To Know

Let’s find out “What Illness Does Cherie Gil Have?” According to reports, cancer was the actress Cherie Gil’s cause of death. She was 59 years old when she died on August 5, 2022.

Actress from the Philippines named Cherie Gil. She earned the nickname “La Primera Contravida” for her acting prowess and contributions to Philippine cinema. Over the course of a nearly 50-year career, Gil earned the reputation of being a “quintessential kontrabida” in Philippine cinema.

Gil has won a FAMAS Award, the Ani ng Dangal (Harvest of Honor) award from the National Commission for Culture and the Arts, and the Metro Manila Film Festival Hall of Fame in the category of “Best Supporting Actress.”

She began her career as a child actor at the age of nine and is most remembered for her portrayal of Lavinia Arguelles in Bituing Walang Ningning, in which she said to Dorina, Sharon Cuneta’s character, “You’re nothing but a second-rate, trying hard copycat!”

Let us learn more about Cherie Gill and take a closer look at her cause of death and illness.

What Illness Does Cherie Gil Have?

Cherie Gil’s illness was cancer which took her life recently.

According to Rappler, the actress was receiving treatment at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center in New York.

She made regular posts about her illness and updates on her Facebook account, she shared every step of her treatment with her fans and followers.

Gil might have been battling cancer for a long period but details about the stage of cancer she was diagnosed and receiving treatment for is not yet available on the web.

Is Actress Cherie Gil Death By Cancer?

Although some websites suggest Cherie Gil’s cause of death is cancer, there are no confirmed reports.

The actress passed away due to her cancer on August 5, 2022.

Cherie’s death was confirmed by her long-term friend and talent manager Annabelle Rama, she shared the news on her Twitter handle with her followers.

She had shaved her hair back in February 2022 while moving to the United States of America.

Gil later clarified the reason for shaving her head to Mega Magazine by saying “I was getting tired of myself. And I was just so angry and unhappy, so I sold everything and packed up”

Meet Cherie Gil’s Husband And Children

Cherie Gil was married to her husband Rony Rogoff in the past.

The couple got married back in 1994 but their marriage ended in 2008 according to her Wikipedia profile.

Gil and Rogoff shared two children together in their 14 years of marriage. The names of their two children are Bianca and Raphael.

Rony was a violinist but many details about his personal life have not come to the surface, similarly, the couple also did not share much about the reason for ending their marriage after 14 years.

Cherie also has another child from her previous relationship, she shares a son Jeremiah David with her former partner actor Leo Martinez.

However, the couple’s relationship timeline and information about how their relationship ended remain fairly unknown in the public domain.

Who Are Cherie Gil’s Father And Mother?

Cherie Gil was born to her father Eddie Mesa and her mother Rosemarie Gil.

Both of her parents are actors and have made a great impact on the Philipino cinema industry.

They are still alive and might be mourning the death of their daughter as you read this article.

Other than her parents, Gil’s family includes her siblings Michael Edward aka Michael de Mesa, and Raphael John aka Mark Gil.
