Akana Hotels , Minna, Nigeria

Akana Hotels and Suites is a large exquisite hotel situated at 75 Gidan Madara road, by City gate, Shango, Minna, Niger state.
The hotel has spacious, beautiful designed rooms, all of which are fully air conditioned. The rooms in the hotel feature a comfy couch, relaxing large beds and a neat personal bathroom with bathtubs. Each room is also accompanied with a refrigerator and television set for entertainment. The onsite restaurant offers a large menu of local delicacies for the guests to choose from. The bar also offers alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages to compliment your meal.
There is adequate parking and security is guaranteed all day long in the hotel.
