Carroll Dunham wiki- bio, age, and height

This re-known painter was born to Carroll Dunham IV and Carol Marguerite Dunham on November 5, 1949. He spent his childhood together with his family in New Haven, Connecticut.

Upon finishing his high-school, artist Carroll Dunham enrolled in Trinity College for the undergraduate program. There he received his BA in 1972.

Currently, he is represented by Gladstone Gallery in New York, Blum & Poe in Los Angeles, Gerhardsen Gerner in Berlin and Galerie Eva Presenhuber in Switzerland. Carroll is best-known around the world for his artworks like Bather/Night, Age of Rectangles, Ship etc.

Painter Carroll Dunham lives in New York city with his wife. As to his age, Carroll Dunham is already in his late 60s; his age was 69 years in last November.
