AJ Styles vs. Edge Announced For WWE WrestleMania 38

and will meet in the ring at WWE WrestleMania 38.

To close out tonight’s episode of WWE Raw, Edge made his way down into the ring to see who would answer his challenge for a match at WrestleMania. Out came Styles, who quickly accepted the challenge. Edge said that he was glad it was Styles, and that he’s hoping to get “the real AJ Styles” in the ring, not someone who’s been playing tag team in WWE.

"I ACCEPT." – @AJStylesOrg @EdgeRatedR has a #WrestleMania opponent … a PHENOMENAL @WrestleMania opponent. pic.twitter.com/pvC41gk3fD

— WWE (@WWE) March 1, 2022

Edge then held his hand out for Styles, and as Styles went to shake it, Edge blasted him with a punch and started attacking him. Styles eventually fought back and dropped Edge with a kick, but “The Rated-R Superstar” was able to avoid the Phenomenal Forearm and hit Styles below the belt.

After dropping him with some elbows, Edge eventually began to leave, before coming back to grab some chairs. He then set up Styles’ head in between both chairs before connecting on a con-chair-to. The WWE Hall of Famer then blasted “The Phenomenal One” with another con-chair-to to end the show.

WWE subsequently confirmed the bout for WrestleMania 38.
