Early Life

Born on April 22, 1986, in Austin, Texas, to Patricia Paige and David Clinton Heard, Amber dropped out of high school at 16 and completed her education through home-schooling.

From 2008-12, she dated the photographer, Tasya van Ree. She met actor Johnny Depp when they were filming The Rum Diary. The two began living together in 2012 and were eventually married in 2015.

In 2015, they were found to be in breach of Australia’s biosecurity laws when they failed to declare that they were travelling with their Yorkshire Terriers to Australian Customs. Amber pleaded guilty to falsifying documents and had to pay a fine as well.

In 2016, she filed for divorce, citing emotional and physical abuse at the hands of her husband. She even got a restraining order against him. Their divorce was finalized in 2017, and Heard withdrew both the restraining request and her previous statements.

Her relationship with Elon Musk lasted only a year as it ended in 2017.
